# Resistance Note

The Resistance Note is a piece of Literature. It can be found on the roof of the Rollermill.

## In-game Description

A formal 'Permission of Public Gathering'. It contains a lot of buzzwordy nonsense, making it difficult to parse.

## Effects

Equipping the Resistance Note causes the Cleric to get a +1 to their Strength and a -2 to their Wisdom.
