# Sir Triban

Sir Triban is a black cat located in South Tolstad near Our Lady's Apartments.

Using the spell Speak with Animals, The Cleric learns that his full title is Sir Triban of the Stones-in-the-Waters. He comes from the Undercoast, residing in "wandering chicken" with his "home-stay servant", an "eccentric woman", likely referring to a witch akin to real world folk character Baba Yaga.
