# Unluck Blade

The Unluck Blade is a Weapon located in the Undercoast Path. It is dropped by the Assassin after they are defeated for the final time.

## In-game Description

One of those ancient daggers that once had the capability to cast 'Wish'. All the charges are spent, but the esoteric energies inside are still rumbling, causing a tiny Esoteric Pocket to rage all around in it.

While holding the dagger, you perform the exact opposite action of the one you intend to perform. You can say yes when you mean no. Attempting to stab the blade in the air causes you to instead shove it into your own stomach. So let's just keep it holstered. For now.

## Effects

Equipping the Unluck Blade grants the Cleric a +3 to their Dexterity and a -1 to their Constitution. Additionally, it provides advantage on checks made against Lawful creatures.
