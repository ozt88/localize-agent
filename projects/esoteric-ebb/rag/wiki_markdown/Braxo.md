# Braxo

Braxo is an imp legal scribe in Tolstad, and a minor but helpful character in the demo. He sits on a pillar south-west of Visken's Lair.

## Description

Braxo is an imp, the lowest-rank Lawful Evil fiend. He claims to not complain, enjoying his social standing and role in Norvikian society. He works as a scribe and legal consultant, contracted by nobles, city officials and similar institutions, and as such has a lucrative business.

He is forthcoming when Cleric asks about the city, world, and society at large, and is a great source for lore. The information he gives, however, is colored by his self-serving tendencies and duplicitous personality.

## Background

He came to the city possibly several decades ago or more. It is uncertain how he came to his low standing in Hells' hierarchy, but in Norvik he has connections to the Palace and as such mingles with the elite of of human society.

He weasels out of saying who he votes for, though comments on Cleric's choice, only reacting positively to the Party of Norvik. As someone relying on the current establishment, it is the party most sensible for him, though he predicts Freestriders to win.
