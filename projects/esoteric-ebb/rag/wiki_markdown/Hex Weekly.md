# Hex Weekly

Hex Weekly is a piece of Literature than can be purchased from Arn for 20 Crowns. It can also be pickpocketed from him at DC 10.

## In-game Description

Hex Weekly is one of those really old magazines that survived through the Coast War. It focuses on the home, family, and lives of women, providing them with health advice and hints on how to feel good at any age. You don't get much out of it, honestly, except maybe a better understanding of the female perspective.

Just looking at it makes you feel a bit emasculated.

## Effect

Equipping Hex Weekly grants the Cleric a +2 to their Wisdom and a -3 to their Strength.
