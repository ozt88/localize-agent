# Sending Pebble

The Sending Pebble is a Trinket found in the dark room on the ground floor of the Rollermill. It can be used to send short messages to various other characters.

## In-game Description

The cost and spell slot requirement of 'Sending' has created many off-brand versions capable of short-distance messaging. During the Coast War, the Arcanist forces were often equipped with this so-called 'Pebble', a stone capable of casting Sending on any familiar target within a kilometer.

## Effects

Equipping the Sending Pebble grants the Cleric a +1 to their Wisdom and Charisma.
