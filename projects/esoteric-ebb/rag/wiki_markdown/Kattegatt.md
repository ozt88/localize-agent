# Kattegatt

= Kattegatt =

Kattegatt is a Weapon found on the wall of the Askanii-Reeds Trading Company Building in Waterlane. It can be stolen with a DC 19 Dexterity check. It resembles a Zweihander that has curled up on itself.

A DC 15 Wisdom check lets the player understand why the blade keeps curling up. Succeeding on the check reveals that the blade is actually sentient, and that it curls up because it has been used to do horrible things in the past. After this check it is possible to attempt to prove yourself to the blade. Doing so will prompt it to ask 5 questions, in an attempt to determine the Cleric's moral character. These questions are, in order:
* "Thy family starves. Two other families also starve. Thou hast food enough for one family only. The other families are smaller. They need but half thy portion. What dost thy heart desire?"
* "Thou findest a purse of gold. No witness observes thee. What dost thy heart desire in this moment?"
* "An innocent is condemned for thy crime. Thou canst save them by confession, losing thy freedom. What doth thy heart yearn for?"
* "A dying stranger begs for water. Thou hast but one flask. The journey ahead is long and perilous. What dost thy heart feel toward this stranger?"
* "Thou hast witnessed great suffering in thy travels. How doth thy heart respond to the pain of others?"
Answering these 5 questions with empathy will satisty Kattegatt, causing it to transform into Moralizing Kattegatt.

Answering the questions without empathy will lead Kattegatt to change in line with your selfishness, causing it to transform into Undermining Kattegatt.

## In-game Description

A sentient vorpal sword, with obvious performance anxiety.

As you hold it in your hand, you hear a faint whisper.

## Effects

Equipping Kattegatt grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength, Dexterity, and Wisdom.

= Moralizing Kattegatt =

Moralizing Kattegatt is a Weapon obtained after answering Kattegatt's questions with empathy.

## In-game Description

A sentient vorpal sword, refusing to spill blood.

It tested you, and found you a good person, for whatever that's worth.

## Effects

Equipping Moralizing Kattegatt grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength, Dexterity, and Wisdom, and gives them advantage against Evil characters.

= Undermining Kattegatt =

Undermining Kattegatt is a Weapon obtained after answering Kattegatt's questions without empathy.

## In-game Description

A sentient vorpal sword. It tested you, and found you a bad person, or whatever that means.

It sadly took after your selfish nature, so it refuses to help you out in any way. Looks cool though.

## Effects

Equipping Undermining Kattegatt grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength, Dexterity, and Wisdom, and gives them advantage against Good characters.
