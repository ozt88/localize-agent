# Three Hundred Pieces

Three Hundred Pieces Ye is minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Esoteric Ebb.

## Walkthrough

Once you've awoken in the morgue, you will see a body to your left. Interacting with it will cause you to realize it could be revived with the necromantic spell Revivify.

## Quest Record

You encountered a corpse in the Lichhouse. It seems fresh and ready for revivify. Though it'd cost quite a bit of money to bring him back - almost a month's rent!
