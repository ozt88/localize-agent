# An Easy Riddle

An Easy Riddle is a major quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Esoteric Ebb.

## Walkthrough

After completing her first riddle, Olzis will offer up her second one:

Found within ARCANE flare.

Twin tongues of flame that scorch the air.

Burrowed deep within the earth,

A PRIMAL string to show your worth.

Folks and gods in thronging herds,

A man DIVINE, alone, howls his words..."}}

{{Spoiler|The vault itself is effectively a void. Go to the right and then upwards to find your reward - the Deck of Many Things, an extremely valuable (and extremely dangerous) magical item.} Once you pick it up, you will temporarily black out, and Olzis will bring you back to the Tavern, completing the quest.}}

## Quest Record

The drunk sphinx in the Drunk Sphinx has offered you another riddle: if you solve it, you gain access to her vault. Are you up to the challenge?
