# Golden Line

Golden Line is a minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Capital Dominion  and Copper Press Fever.

## Quest Record

There's a geopolitical conflict going on in the clanlands. The homeland of the Askanii-native halflings has been cut in half - by the so-called Golden Line. Celestials hold the west. Maybe you should find out more?
