# The Lost 6th Edition

The Lost 6th Edition is a minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Esoteric Ebb (Quest).

## Walkthrough

To start The Lost 6th Edition, you will need to speak with Snurre Appledent.

## Quest Record

Sch. Snurre Appledent has tasked you with retrieving a rare copy of Cividix's Book of the Mind, which is supposedly found somewhere in the dungeons of the City Below.
