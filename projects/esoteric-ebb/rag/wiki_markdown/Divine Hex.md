# Divine Hex

Divine Hex is a Spell. It is obtained by forming a Warlock Pact with Ever-Glad.

## Effects

You place a curse on a creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, every roll against this creature is increased by your proficiency.

If the target drops to 0 Hit Points before this spell ends, you can take a Bonus Action on a later turn to curse a new creature. Since you're so good, this will be done automatically.
