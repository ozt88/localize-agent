# Antesepalian

"Antesepalian" means "before the closing of the gates", used to describe civilization during the Gate Era, before Jor closed the portals to The Coast, 2027 years before the events of the game.

The term is comprised of the Latinate prefix ante- meaning "prior to", sepalian of uncertain origins (possibly sēparō, meaning "to separate"). It is similar to the biblical term antediluvian, meaning "before the flood".
