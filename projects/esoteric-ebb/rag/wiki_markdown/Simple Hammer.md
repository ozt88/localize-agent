# Simple Hammer

The Simple Hammer is a Weapon. It can be found on the floor outside of Visken's Lair after resolving Circle Square Conflict.

## In-game Description

A simple hammer. Could be useful in a manly pinch. Not good for a fight though.

## Effects

Equipping the Simple Hammer grants the Cleric +1 to their Constitution, and gives them advantage in situations where they might need a hammer.
Situations where the Cleric gains advantage:
* Chiseling off name from statue of Urth
* Smashing open the planks to enter the Tea Shop (-4 to DC)
* Forcing the door at the bottom of the Rollermill open

## Trivia

The Hammer is wielded by the Cleric in art of the Constitution focused Pre-built Beefy Cleric.
