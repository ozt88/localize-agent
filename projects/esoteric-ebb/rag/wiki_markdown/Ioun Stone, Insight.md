# Ioun Stone, Insight

The Ioun Stone, Insight is a Trinket given by a Brrr after saving him in the Undercoast Path.

## Description

A legandary magic item, probably produced during the Arcane Era. It's got the markings of Old Diminoran craft. It floats around your head as you walk around.

## Effects

Equipping the Ioun Stone, Insight, grants the Cleric a +3 to their Wisdom.
