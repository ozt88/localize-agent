# Arcanists

Arcanists are a magocratic (i.e. autocracy of mages) political ideology, movement, and party that centers around anti-democratic rule of the most powerful wizards. Arcanist is a term which refers to the magocrats who started the aristocratic families that ruled Reeds until the Coast War.

## Arcanists as a political party

There are two names of recognized Arcanist parties.

Arcanist, Party of Magic appears on the Voting Card. Intelligence notes it as a very minor party.

The Arcane Circle, which is run by Rubrick Prax, who is the last of the magocratic family of House Prax. Charisma claims most people treat them as a joke. In polling they earn only half of a percent of votes. They are based in Rotating Tower in Ôstgârd.
