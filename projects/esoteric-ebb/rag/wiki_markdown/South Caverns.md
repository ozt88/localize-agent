# South Caverns

The South Caverns are a sub-section of the City Below the City. They are inhabited by a Roper, and are located in the domain of the HWBT.

## Description

The caverns are accessed through passage in the Roots. An exit to The Sewers can be found within the Caverns, but is blocked off by a hidden Roper.

Multiple ancient relics from Askanii's past can be found in the caverns. A cave painting dating back to before the Toulin'catl can be found in the caverns. A Shrine made by the Whitemane can be found in the caverns as well. If the Jacinth is inserted into this Shrine, a trap will activate, and the Spectral Halberd can be obtained.

The headless corpse of a rogue can be found in the caverns, from which the Esoteric Lockpick can be taken.
