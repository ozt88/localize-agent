# Snell

Snell is Cleric's goblin companion investigating the tea shop explosion on behalf of the HWBT. He is a possible party member, and the one most likely to be encountered first.

## Description

Charisma describes him as superficial, based on how well he dresses.

When dealing with The Cleric, he is often sarcastic, resorting to mockery with irony on every opportunity. Though he's antagonistic towards The Cleric, he will help Cleric when he's in danger (excluding certain encounters, where the Cleric is facing high odds or is the aggressor to undeserving characters) and will answer any question pertaining to the case and Tolstad.

As a local, his role is to chime in with insight on Tolstad and its people. As of the demo, he tries to avoid personal questions. To convince him to talk about what party he will vote for, the Cleric must pass Charisma check DC 18.

He holds much loyalty and reverence for Lady Sageleaf.

## Background

Snell grew up in Tolstad, brought up in a goblin nursery in Lower Tolstad. At some point in time, he was a registered Freestrider. As with other goblins, he lives in the Holes.

He works as an agent of the HWBT. From his notepad the player learns that he's been tasked with investigating the Tea shop, and it was him who suggested to get an arcane cleric involved.
