# Bands

Eight esoterically divided geographical areas that makes up The Coast. Each band is distinct in both geography and inhabitants. The size of the sun is inconsistent between separate Bands.

Each band stretched from the ocean to the Infinite Wastes.

The Journal definition of a Band appears after succeeding a DC 9 Intelligence check and reads "Band - an esoteric divide splits the circular Coast of Jor into eight geographical areas, commonly known as 'bands'. Each band has unique esoteric and natural traits, and stretches from the Coast to the Infinite Wastes."

## List of Bands

* Reeds
* Vourgeni
* Hae'Xi
* Kargamesh
* Fell Tire
* Ym
* Divinii Torum
* Askanii
