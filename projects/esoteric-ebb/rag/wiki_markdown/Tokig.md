# Tokig

Tokig the Gambler was a trickster god and an ascended goblin.

## Description

Tokig, like other gods, used to be a mortal, specifically a goblin. Wisdom notes that his existence was regarded as a "silly goblin myth", though that information might be dubious based on the attitude of other races towards goblins.

During the Divine Era, he united goblin tribes of Askan. Wisdom states that he cut off his hand to fulfill a prophecy, the details of which are not known in the demo.

As a trickster god, prayers dedicated to him are expected to not come into fruition, more often doing the opposite. There are few goblins that worship him.

The Journal definition of Tokig the Gambler appears after succeeding a DC 13 Wisdom check and reads: "Tokig the Gambler - a trickster goblin who united the tribes of Askanii during the Divine Era. Most famous for cutting off his right hand to fulfill a prophecy."
