# Order of Arcane Clerics

The Order of Arcane Clerics of Norvik (abbr. OACN, also known as The Towers) are a section of the Urthguard which specializes in spellcasting. The Cleric is part of the order. Their headquarters are called The Towers.

The order is made up of Clerics who study magic and are tasked with investigating incidents related to spellcasting and esoteric events. They are one of the few authorized spellcaster groups.
