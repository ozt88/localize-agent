# Phoenix Folio

Phoenix Folio is a minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of quest Class Conflict.

## Walkthrough

When you're speaking with Visken after waking up, he will mention that your Spellbook was seriously damaged during your time underwater. Picking it up will start the quest.

Take it to Waterlane, and go to the north side of town. The Bookbinder lives under the office of Snurre Appledent. You will have to wait until the evening or night to speak with him, as he is nocturnal. While you speak with him, he tells you that he can either remake the book as it originally was or modify it depending on your current needs.

The Prayer Book will give you +2 Intelligence, and will add your proficiency to the maximum amount of prepared spells (when equipped).

The Green Ledger will give you +2 Wisdom, and will add your proficiency to the maximum amount of prepared spells (when equipped).

The Dark Grimoire will give you +2 Charisma, and will add your proficiency to the maximum amount of prepared spells (when equipped).

Returning to the Bookbinder the following afternoon will allow you to pick up the book, but you will have to pay him 250 crowns before he returns it to you. By passing a high charisma check, you can convince him to lower the price to 100 crowns in exchange for you spreading good word about his business. Recovering your repaired book will finish the quest.

## Quest Record

After your river adventure, your old spellbook is ruined! Find a service provider who can rebind it for you. Fix your ruined spellbook, damn it!
