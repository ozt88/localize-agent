# Pinja

Pínja'Athena is an enigmatic elf found behind a house in Tolstad North, silently looking at River Torna. Pinja speaks in cryptic, wistful phrases, and as such it is hard to characterize her.

## Background

The "Athena" part of her name is translated as "of the Mend", and is a title given to those related to Illym The Mender.

After passing a DC 5 Strength check, Pinja reveals that her unnamed friend needs help, while also showing antagonism towards the paladin Gorm Skaldeman.
