# Light Crossbow

The Light Crossbow is a Weapon belonging to Ôst. It can be obtained by convincing the Kobold to give it to you, or by pickpocketing him.

## In-game Description

A finely crafted crossbow. Lightweight. The type used by peasantry in the late years of the Coast War. It's a tool of swift and easy death for those without training or cause.

The mechanism appears to be jammed, however. So you can't fire it. But it sure looks intimidating.

## Effects

Equipping the Light Crossbow grants the Cleric +1 to their Dexterity and Wisdom.
