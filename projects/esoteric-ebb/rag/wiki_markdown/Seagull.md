# Seagull

A giant seagull can be found in the northernmost bridge of Tolstad.

The Cleric can engage with the Seagull in multiple ways to obtain the item Plume of Righteousness. Dexterity check of DC 10 to pet the Seagull initiates Combat. The Pickpocketing DC for the item is 16.

With the spell Speak with Animals, The Cleric learns that the seagull's name is Fei. Fei claims to be "Servant of the Saint See", seemingly akin to knight or a paladin. The Holy See seems to be a seagull religious figure. The seagull mentions others related to the faith: Lord of the Water, Stringmaster, and the Feathered Court. They may imply that seagulls are the favored creature of Jor.
