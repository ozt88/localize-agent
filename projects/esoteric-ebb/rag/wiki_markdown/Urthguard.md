# Urthguard

Urthguard in Norvik are the main law-keeping organization; the guards of the city. They are linked to the religion of the god Urth. The Cleric is part of the Urthguard, specifically the Order of Arcane Clerics.

The Journal definition of Urthguard appears after succeeding a DC 10 Strength check and reads: "Urthguard - the heroes of Norvik, keepers of city law, defenders of humanity. Your brothers."

## History

Borgo Darrow, the first Urth paladin, founded the Urthguard.
