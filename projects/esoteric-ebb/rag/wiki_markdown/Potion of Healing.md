# Potion of Healing

Potions of Healing are healing Consumables found in various places around Tolstad and the City Below. One Potion of Healing can be found in a crate of potions in The Sewers.

They are sold by the Hexbrew Corporation, a Hag Mega-coven.

## In-game Description

A standard potion of healing. The label says, HEXBREW INKORPORATION, 6 PW.
