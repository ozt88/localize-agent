# Esoteric Pockets

Pockets of space where various different Esoteric effects apply. They are rarely found around the Coast, but become increasingly prevalent the closer one gets to the Infinite Wastes.

The Journal definition of an Esoteric Pocket appears after succeeding a DC 18 Intelligence check and reads: "Esoteric Pocket - area of weird magic. Seldom found along the Coast. They have been studied and categorized extensively. The most common ones are Biome Pockets, One-Rule Pockets, and Dread Pockets."
