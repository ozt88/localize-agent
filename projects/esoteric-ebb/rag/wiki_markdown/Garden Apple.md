# Garden Apple

Garden Apple is a healing consumable. It is the basic healing item, healing only 1 HP.

It is the most commonly found item, scattered across Tolstad, as well as the earliest healing item, because The Cleric starts in Visken's Lair, where the apples are stored en masse.

Apples have a significance for the economy of Tolstad, being the main product that the HWBT produces and sells, theirs being the only apple farm in Norvik.

They also function as a motif throughout the game.

* The game begins proper in Visken's Lair, surrounded by apples, and after Cleric takes damage during the intro, the Abilities suggest healing.
* An early Ability Check concerns the apple pile, which when succeeding not only heal the Cleric, but award an early spell
* Constitution references apples, eating being part of its stomach and hunger theme.
* The Background Focus Punching Bag (where Cleric is said to be son of a farmer) uses the apple as its icon.
* The Constitution-focused Pre-built Beefy Cleric features art of The Cleric lying on a pile of apples, with the caption "Yes. More apples, Appleboy," which Constitution says in the game.
* When Nessan asks if Cleric eats well, The Cleric may reference eating plenty of apples.
* The Dungeons & Dragons spell Goodberry is replaced by Goodapple, though the item the spell produces is a goodapple.

## In-game Description

An Apple grown on Tolstad. The colors ripple across the surface. Consuming it will save your soul.
