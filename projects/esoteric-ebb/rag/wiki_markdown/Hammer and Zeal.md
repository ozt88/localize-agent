# Hammer and Zeal

Hammer and Zeal is minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Norvikian Resistance and Copper Press Fever.

## Walkthrough

Your choice will impact their dialogue on election day. Once you've made a decision on who to support, the conversation finishes up, and the quest ends.}}
