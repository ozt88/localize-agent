# Gorm Skaldeman

Gorm Skaldeman is the last appointed paladin of Urth. He became the face of the Party Of Norvik, but went missing months prior to the events of the game. The quest Missing Paladin is concerned with this.

## Background

Due to his position of prominence in the Party of Norvik, Gorm was able to learn the truth of Lord Patriarch Âkesam's condition that the magistrates were keeping from the populace: that he had been Feebleminded 3 years prior to the events of the game. This, combined with the knowledge he had that the Magistrates were selling parts of the city to the Freestriders, led him to a desperate conclusion: Urth had to return.

Gorm ventured into the Undercoast Path to find Ever-Glad and sign a Pact with him, gaining knowledge on a spell that would supposedly resurrect Urth. He traveled to the Curselands to gather 5 branches from trees from Urth's homeland. He convinced the Illym that he was a traitor working against the rest of Norvik to gain their financial backing, letting him obtain the vast amount of silver and diamonds required for the spell. For the final component of the spell, the souls of 27 of Urth's loyal followers, Gorm intended to use a Necklace of Fireballs he received from the Illym elves to cause an explosion in the Secret Tunnel on Election Day.

On the 21st of March, upon learning that Pinja, the elf who held the necklace, intended to sell it to a Freestrider Agent and flee the city, Gorm confronted her in the Tea Shop. He attempted to cast Charm Person on her and failed, causing her to panic and threaten to detonate the Necklace. The conversation further escalated, eventually culminating in Pinja detonating the Necklace. Gorm cast Misty Step to escape right outside the shop, suffering  only minor burns. He turned himself invisible and entered the Pillar of Jor, being spotted by Meriadoc Moonwatch.

Pinja, expressing worry about her friend, says that Gorm is a danger to her and points to his whereabouts.
