# Reeds

Reeds is one of the eight bands along The Coast. It is viewed as the homeland of Humans.

It was known as Diminor during the Arcane Era, and is still often referred to as Old Diminor. The name was changed by Freestrider revolutionaries during the Coast War Capitulation Agreements to signify the end of Magocracy, since the old name comes from the magocratic family Diminorii.

The geography is of temperate lowlands with many lakes. Large portions of the land are cursed; the effects include creation of the undead (entire cities can be filled with them), eternally burning valleys, and floating mountains.

The Journal definition of Reeds appears after succeeding a DC 13 Dexterity check and reads: "Reeds - the leftovers. The center of the Coast War, which ripped the entire band apart. Today, it's a shining beacon of everything modern and pretty and new and so on."

### Reedsian Seasons

* Reedsian Spring - 1st of Felteni
* Reedsian Summer - 1st of Beltan
* Reedsian Autumn - 1st of Thusi
* Reedsian Winter - 1st of Soumil
