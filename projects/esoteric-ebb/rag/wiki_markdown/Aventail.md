# Aventail

Aventail is a Helmet looted from the body of the Skeleton.

## In-game Description

Taken from an undead in the City Below. This helm was worn in the early days of Norvik, by those powerful spellslinging clerics who served Urth in his reconquest. It even has some glowing, esoteric runes left on the inside:

Berg-grûnden skakar av er makt, dû trollkarl af Urth. Mâ marken för evigt vara dîn.

## Effects

Equipping Aventail grants the Cleric +1 to their Intelligence and Constitution, and -2 to their Charisma.

## Trivia

The runes in Aventail's description are Swedish for: "The very foundations of the mountains tremble with your power, you sorcerer of Urth. May the land forever be yours."

The Aventail is worn by the Cleric in the Constitution focused Pre-built Beefy Cleric.
