# Ûrth-Nisse

Ûrth-Nisse is a member of the Norvikian Resistance. He can usually be found in the Rollermill or Tavern with his friends.

## Description

Ûrth dresses in red, and has dark red hair.

## Background

Ûrth-Nisse seems to have some minor magic proficiency, a fact which is brought up repeatedly. He is often seen casting fire cantrips. While speaking to Rollo about the employment status of himself and the other members of the Norvikian resistance, Ûrth mentions that he used to deliver milk. If the Cleric tries to convince the resistance to join the Urthguard, Ûrth says his father would be happy if he had consistent employment.
