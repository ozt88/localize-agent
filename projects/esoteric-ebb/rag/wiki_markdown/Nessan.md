# Nessan

Nes'Wikkala (also known as Nessan) is a Pimeä Elves druid that came to Tolstad from the Undercoast. She resides in the Goblin Garden

Attempting to pickpocket from Nessan and failing causes her to cast Thorn Whip on the Cleric. You make a DC 15 passive Dexterity check. Failing causes the Cleric to take 8d4 damage, while succeeding results in you successfully dodging and taking 4d4 damage instead.

## Description

Nessan is an eccentric, but kind and wise druid concerned primarily with health and balance of nature. She speaks with a heavy Undercoast accent and often repeats her words.

She is readily helpful, teaching The Cleric about the world, magic, and druidry, providing the spell Speak With Animals after passing a Wisdom check.

## Background

She introduces herself as an underling to the Hierophant Ohpidana

She has a platonic relationship with Lord Puu, who is a tree. The spell Goodapple (which she has in the demo) will be found near the tree, given to Moongore, HWBT's enforcer.
