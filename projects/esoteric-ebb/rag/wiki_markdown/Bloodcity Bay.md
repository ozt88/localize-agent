# Bloodcity Bay

Blodcity Bay is the largest city of Fell Tire. From the little information in the game, it seems to be home of Evil-aligned people such as Devils or Orcs.

The city was built on top of a corpse of the Archdevil Gorgorian. Buildings were carved into the remains, augmented with wooden passageways.

The city's currency are called Brimmarks, which are either banknotes or soul-infused iron coins.

Viira was born in Bloodcity Bay, where she was involved in a warband.
