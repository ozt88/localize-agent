# Piece of Paper

The Piece of Paper is a piece of Literature found on a corpse in the City Below the City.

Examining the Piece of Paper reveals a section on it written in Draconic. It reads: "Dominus noster mox redibit, posteri eius insidiantur. Magnus Thesaurus Esuthi intra muros sedis amissae iacet, sub pedibus proditorum. Spiritum filii eius primogeniti inveni et cum eo locutus sum. Ossa eius captiva in illis arcanis aulis tenentur, praesentia spurcae ducis goblinorum stabilita. Quamdiu illa terram illam tenet, divinae reliquiae eius non movebuntur - sed ingressum obsignavi. Solum vero sectatori aperietur. Ave, Esuthu! Ignis Litus Verrat!"

A DC 12 Intelligence check can reveal the translation of the Draconic text.

## In-game Description

Some kind of adventuring log.

## Effects

Equipping the Piece of Paper grants the Cleric +1 to their Dexterity and -2 to their Strength.
