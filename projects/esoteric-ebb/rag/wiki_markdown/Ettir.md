# Ettir

Ettir is a city-sworn angel of Tolstad, in service of Magister Darrow. She can be found in front of his house, highest part of Tolstad North.

## Description

Ettir is a stern and stoic representative of the celestials, according to Intelligence likely a member of the Golden Horde.

She says only what is necessary, and doesn't seem to want to say even that. When she is confronted with the more pathetic side of The Cleric, she either ignores him or dismisses it with biting remarks, sometimes in the Celestial language (which in Esoteric Ebb is Polish).

She is shown to be highly powerful and capable. She communicates telepathically and can be extremely intimidating towards The Cleric, though it is uncertain how much of that is due to her celestial nature. She speaks all languages, including the Goblin language, despite it (at least locally) being a dead language.
