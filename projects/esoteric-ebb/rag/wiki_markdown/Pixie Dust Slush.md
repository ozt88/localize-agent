# Pixie Dust Slush

Pixie Dust Slush is a consumable that increases the Cleric's Dexterity by +1 for a week. 4 Pixie Dust Slushes can be found around Tolstad: one can be stolen from a cupboard within the Askanii-Reeds Trading Company Building, one can be pickpocketed at DC 14 from Miska Sageleaf, one can be found in Darrow's Nest, in a desk drawer that can be detected by Detect Secrets, and one can be bought from the Potion Seller for 250 Crowns.

## In-game description

Popular in Reeds. A glittering mess, ready to get your reflexes up to snuff.

## Effect

Consuming the Pixie Dust Slush causes the Cleric's Dexterity to increase by +1 for a week.
