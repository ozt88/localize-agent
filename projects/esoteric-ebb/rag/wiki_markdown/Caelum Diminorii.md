# Caelum Diminorii

Caelum Diminorii was a member of the Diminorii and a legendary figure. He was known as the First Arcanist. Whether he was a real person or not is a topic of hot dispute.

It was said that Caelum learned the secrets of magic from Dragonmasters.

Caelum was the author of De Verbis Draconum, Ars Occulta.
