# Olzis

Olzis is a Gynosphinx who lives in the Drunk Sphinx tavern. She is the tavern's namesake.

Olzis is the guardian of a Divine Vault, located behind her in the tavern.

Asking Olzis about Sphinx reproduction and listening to the tale of her first assignment can lead to her giving you 4x Incense.

If asked for a riddle, Olzis will say the following:

"Found within ARCANE flare.

Twin tongues of flame that scorch the air.

Burrowed deep within the earth,

A PRIMAL string to show your worth.

Folks and gods in thronging herds,

A man DIVINE, alone, howls his words..."

## Description

Olzis harbors great disdain towards male sphinxes, with her believing that lesser sphinxes are mindless imbeciles, and that Androsphinxes are prideful and insufferable.

## Background

Olzis' first mission after being sent to the Realm of Jor was to guard the border of a long forgotten empire. In this task she was accompanied by an Androsphinx. She and this other sphinx had a relationship, which increasingly soured over time, eventually resulting in the two of them parting ways.

Olzis has been in the area taken up by Tolstad since the Fade Era, over a millennium ago. During this time period she and the Toulin'catl frequently interacted, with the snailfolk bargaining with her for divine knowledge and assistance.
