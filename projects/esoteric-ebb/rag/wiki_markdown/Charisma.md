# Charisma

Charisma (abbr. CHA) is a character statistic, and a character in The Cleric's mind.

It dictates social prowess and represents the strength of one's personality.

## Mechanics

For more detailed information about abilities and dice rolls see Abilities.

Charisma is rolled in social contests. Though persuasiveness can call for any ability, Charisma is specific to lies and other deceptive forms of persuasion, as well as the ability to keep a straight face under pressure, and expressing one's self in appropriate manner.

In Dungeons & Dragons, Charisma is also used as a spellcasting modifier for classes other than cleric, whose modifier is Wisdom. As such, Charisma can also be seen as the ability of one's will to reform reality.

## Personality

As a chime, Charisma is vain, calculating, and somewhat narcissistic when it comes to beauty or social prowess.

Charisma is supposed to represent the part of Cleric's self that does things for attention and social standing, as evident from the Background Focus Little Weirdo. It is a manipulative personality that wants to play along with what others think. As such, it is an "apolitical" voice in the way that it doesn't have an ideology and only appeals to what other people think.

After interacting with Miss Nearly and passing a passive Charisma check, Charisma will begin to insist that the Cleric is actually a 'Bard', another class in Dungeons & Dragons.
