# Kha-Ymor

Kha-Ymor was a Mist Titan with historical importance during the Gate Era. He was discussed most prominently in the book Folk Tales found in Visken's Lair.

He is described as having "fur as thick as [...] forests", body large enough to clutch mountains, and an upper body obscured by (or made of) mist.

His domain, a fertile valley, was targeted by Esuth. In the folk tale, Kha-Ymor is accompanied by a "half-wit" Abacus who helps him find and slay Esuth.
