# Docile Stress Cube

The Docile Stress Cube is a Trinket obtained after defeating the Gelatinous Cube.

## In-game Description

A translucent somewhat firm gelatinous cube. These used to be sold as toys/pets. Today they're illegal. Probably because they can technically still dissolve human flesh, albeit very, very slowly.

## Effects

Equipping the Docile Stress Cube grants the Cleric +1 to their Wisdom.
