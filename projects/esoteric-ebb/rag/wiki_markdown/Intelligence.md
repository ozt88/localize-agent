# Intelligence

Intelligence (abbr. INT) is a character statistic, and a character in The Cleric's mind.

It represents character's knowledge and ability to process and use it.

Reaching 20 Base Intelligence grants the Cleric an additional Literature slot.

## Mechanics

For more detailed information about abilities and dice rolls see Abilities.

Intelligence is called in dice rolls that require planning, processing information, or anything related to the bookish side of the mind.

It is helpful with Spellcasting, awarding The Cleric with more spell slots. It also is the vehicle for arcane part of magic, the spellcasting ability of Wizards as a class.

## Personality

As a chime, Intelligence often serves as an info-dump. But rather than factual presentation of history, it often steers into its sarcastic, ego-driven commentary. Intelligence is arrogant and convinced of its own superiority over all others. If there is one defining characteristic for Intelligence, it is the belief in the supremacy of the self, and as such it scoffs with many of its remarks.

Intelligence is supposed to represent the extreme side of the Arcanist political party, or rather magocracy, the aristocratic rule of mages. Specifically it views itself as the only rightful ruler of everything, by the merit of its intellect. Where Strength represents the human-centered morality of the strong protecting the weak, Intelligence represents the meritocratic supremacy, the superior ruling the weak and ignorant masses.
