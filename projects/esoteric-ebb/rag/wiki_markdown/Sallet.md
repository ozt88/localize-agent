# Sallet

The Sallet is a Helmet found in the Askanii Reeds Trading Company Warehouse, in a crate of Mosquito Sand.

## Effects

Equipping the Sallet grants the Cleric a +2 to their Dexterity, and a -2 to their Strength.

## Trivia

The Sallet is worn by the Cleric in the art of the Dexterity focused Pre-built Trickster Cleric.
