# Collected Chants of Norvik

The Collected Chants of Norvik is a work of Literature located in on a bookshelf in Visken's Lair. It can read while on the bookshelf. While reading the book, a DC 16 passive Strength check will lead to Strength exclaiming that you must steal the book. After this, the book can be accquired as an item.

## In-game Description

A copy of the Norvikian Chants, with a scribbled message from Pilli himself.

This is a god damn national treasure. Somewhat.

## Effects

Equipping the Collected Chants of Norvik grants the Cleric +2 to their Strength.
