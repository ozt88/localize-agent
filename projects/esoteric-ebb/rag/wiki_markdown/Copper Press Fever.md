# Copper Press Fever

Copper Press Fever is a major quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Young and Dumb and branches into Hammer and Zeal.

## Walkthrough

.

Your choice in this matter will have a mild impact on the events of Day 6. Once your decision is made, the quest will end.

## Quest Record

An Azgalist Newspaper is being printed on Tolstad. You should find the location of this copper press and the name of the editor.

Completing Copper Press Fever will allow you to choose from one of three feats related to the quest.

Mass Media Propaganda - Alters ability score modifiers on TEXTS. Positive modifiers are increased by 1 and negative modifiers are decreased by 1.

Human-Dwarf Unity - While you are at full health, whenever you take damage, those dice are rolled with disadvantage.

Azgalian Endurance - For every 3 hours spent being exhausted, you level 1 level of exhaustion.
