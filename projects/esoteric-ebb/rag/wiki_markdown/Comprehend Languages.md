# Comprehend Languages

Comprehend Languages is a utility spell. It can be purchased from the Storm Gnome vendor for 100 Crowns.

## Effect

For the duration, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language you hear. You also understand any written language that you see, but you must be touching the surface on which the words are written.

This spell doesn't decode secret messages in a text or glyph, such as an arcane sigil, that isn't part of a written language.

### List of Comprehendable Language

* Draconic words on a book in Visken's Lair (Grants Caelum's Mage Hand)
* Toulin'catl inscriptions upon Garden Monument in Tolstad South
* Brrr's speech
