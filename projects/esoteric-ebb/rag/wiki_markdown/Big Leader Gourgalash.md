# Big Leader Gourgalash

Big Leader Gourgalash is a Frost Troll that can be found in the Undercoast Path. He is first encountered while asleep, and will not wake up unless the Cleric approaches or attacks him.

## Background

In 468 AE, Gourgalash was defeated by Urth in the ruins of Toulin'Catl, marking the end of The Conquest.
