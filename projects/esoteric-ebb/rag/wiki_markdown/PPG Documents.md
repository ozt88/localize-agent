# PPG Documents

PPG Documents are a piece of Literature. They can be taken from Alfoz by succeeding on an Intelligence check, or pickpocketed from him at DC 9.

While examining the Documents, a DC 5 Intelligence check can reveal that it is perfectly in order, but is dated to a week ago, making in invalid. This check's DC will be reduced by 1 if Agent Viira has been spoken to.

## In-game Description

A formal 'Permission of Public Gathering'. It contains a lot of legal nonsense, making it difficult to parse.

## Effects

Equipping PPG Documents causes the Cleric to get a -1 to their Intelligence and Wisdom.
