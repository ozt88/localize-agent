# The Mind Expansion

The Mind Expansion is a consumable that increases the Cleric's Intelligence by +1 for a week. Two bottles of The Mind Expansion can be found across Tolstad: one can be pickpocketed at DC 15 from Visken, and one can be bought from the Potion Seller for 290 Crowns.

## In-game description

A common potion known to allow knowledge to stick more easily. Often used by students.

## Effect

Consuming The Mind Expansion causes the Cleric's Intelligence to increase by +1 for a week.
