# Arn

Arn is a human child working at the Pillar Kiosk next to the Pillar of Jor in Tolstad. He sells equipable newspapers and magazines. He is a source of useful information both for quests and for lore.

## Description

Arn is a readily co-operative and easily manipulated character. He isn't comfortable talking to The Cleric but does well assuming a friendly demeanor to sell his stock.

## Background

Arn mentions he has an older brother.

He was employed by Thalassos, a merman who owns multiple properties in Tolstad.
