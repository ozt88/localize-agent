# Vourgeni

Vourgeni is one of the eight bands along The Coast. It neighbors Hae'Xi and Reeds.

Vourgeni is the home of the giants. Because of this, it is known as the land of giants.

Vourgeni's landscape is made up of gravity-defying mountains.

### Vourgeni Seasons

* Vourgeni Spring - 14th of Ingarmi
* Vourgeni Summer - 6th of Gorgoni
* Vourgeni Autumn - 22nd of March
* Vourgeni Winter - 20th of Thisi

## Inhabitants

Vourgeni is the home of a race of people known as the Landbûar. The Landbûar were taller than most humans, due to some amount of interbreeding with giants in the past. They usually had white or black hair, prominent noses, tanned skin, and freckles. The Landbûar lived in cities on the floating hills of Vourgeni. They are known for their talented Primal spellcasters.

A large majority of the Landbûar were forcibly conscripted during the Coast War, and their population has never truly recovered.
