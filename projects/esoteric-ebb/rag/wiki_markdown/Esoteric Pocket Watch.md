# Esoteric Pocket Watch

The Esoteric Pocket Watch is a Trinket found on Visken's desk in Visken's Lair. It is a pocket watch that is perpetually incorrect.

## In-game Description

This silver-lined timepiece never shows the correct time of day. It is always at least ten hours out of alignment, no matter how anyone tries to correct it. At a touch you can feel the faint magic pulsating within. The wielder gains a greater perception of esoteric happenings and occurrences within 200 feet.

## Effects

Equipping the Esoteric Pocket Patch grants the Cleric a +1 to their Intelligence, and gives them advantage in esoteric events. Situations where the Cleric gains advantage:
* Meditating at Pillar of Jor at street level (-2 to DC)
* Pulling on the Strings after falling from short ledge or ladder
* Attempting to communicate with the Goblin Artwork

## Trivia

The Pocket Watch is held by the Cleric in art of the Intelligence focused Pre-built Scholar Cleric.
