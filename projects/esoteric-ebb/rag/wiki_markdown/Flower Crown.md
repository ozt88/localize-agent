# Flower Crown

The Flower Crown is a Trinket obtained from Miss Nearly after completing the quest Essence of Inflorescence, or pickpocketed from her at DC 12.

## In-game Description

A crown of pretty flowers, most likely sourced from local gardens. Gives you an air of home-grown understanding and elderness. Will make it clear that you care about the forest, and all its natural inhabitants.

## Effects

Equipping the Flower Crown grants the Cleric a +1 to their Wisdom, and gives them advantage against plant-based individuals.
