# Druidcraft

Druidcraft is a cantrip that can be obtained from Nessan after passing a DC 20 Wisdom check. The DC is lowered by 5 when Nessan "Finds you reasonable" (based on the answer to her question of sustainability) and by 5 when "She pities you and your hungry stomach" (based on an answer where The Cleric claims he can survive entirely on apples).

## In-game Description

Whispering to the spirits of nature, you create a tiny, harmless sensory effect that follows you. This effect allows you to engage with primal entities that would otherwise hide from your presence.
