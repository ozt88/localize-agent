# Lichhouse Blues

Lichhouse Blues is  major quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches into River Take Ye, Crypt Keeper and Cult Hit.

## Walkthrough

However you decide to complete the final encounter, surviving it for the first time will award you with the achievement "Just a Local Mortician."

## Quest Record

The pale man called Visken is very suspicious. Find out what is going on in the Lichhouse.

Completing Lichhouse Blues will allow you to choose from one of three feats related to the quest.
