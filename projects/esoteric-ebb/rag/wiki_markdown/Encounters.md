# Encounters

Encounters are turn-based conflicts (not necessarily physical combat as in Dungeons & Dragons).

An encounter begins with all participants rolling for Initiative (Dexterity) to determine the order in which the characters perform their actions. The higher the roll, the earlier a character acts. On a tie, The Cleric gets precedence.

The encounter than cycles between each character. The opponents often use an action to damage Cleric's Hit Points, but may react to what the Cleric has done instead. The player gets a narration from Chimes that leads to a choice between few options, each option being an Ability Check with different DC. The options are the strategies of a different Chimes, usually 3 of them. As with other Checks, the Cleric's previous actions may lower or raise the DC of the check.

This is a non-traditional turn-based combat that instead of each character dealing damage with their given actions, The Cleric goes through a dialogue tree, each time having to pass a check.
