# Bronze Shield

The Bronze Shield is a Trinket that can be found in front of an abandoned hut in the Undercoast Path.

## In-game Description

A shield left by one of the predecessors of what is today Norvik. A cat-like eye is displayed on the front, and as you wield it, there's a whisper in your ears. It says something like, Oh, sí..., he de resguardarte, camarada... aun cuando eres bien pelón...

## Effects

Equipping the Bronze Shield grants the Cleric a +1 to their Dexterity and Wisdom.

## Trivia

The phrase whispered by the shield means "Oh, yes... I must protect you, comrade... even if you are quite bald..." in Whitemane (Spanish).
