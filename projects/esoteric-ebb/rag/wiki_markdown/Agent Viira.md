# Agent Viira

Viira is an orc from Bloodcity Bay in the employ of Askanii-Reeds Trading Company, a Freestrider organization. She is found outside of Visken's Lair, south of Alfoz. After completing the quest Circle Square Conflict, she moves into the Askanii-Reeds Trading Company Building until midnight on day one.

## Description

Viira is a sly, somewhat manipulative character who despite her strength chooses to contest others through wit and social mechanisms. At the surface, Viira is amicable and forthcoming, but she will always steer the conversation where she needs to, especially for the benefit of her agenda.

## Background

Viira grew up in the Bloodcity Bay. From a young age, she took part in warbands and became a capable warrior.

During the Revolutionary War in Reeds she was contacted by the Askanii-Reeds Trading Company and fought on the behalf of the Revolutionary Freestriders. After the war, she remained with the company, climbing to the rank of Second-in-Command Guild Agent for Tolstad.
