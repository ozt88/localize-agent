# Stress Cube

The Stress Cube is a Trinket obtained after passing a dexterity check in upper level of the Northwest Tolstad Rollermill.

## In-game Description

A translucent somewhat firm gelatinous cube. These used to be sold as toys/pets. Today they're illegal. Probably because they can technically still dissolve human flesh, albeit very, very slowly.

## Effects

Equipping the Stress Cube grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength.
