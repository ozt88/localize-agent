# Gauntlets of Ogre Power

The Gauntlets of Ogre Power are a Trinket found in the Secret Tunnel.

## In-game Description

A pair of leather-bound steel gauntlets. The material is rough and causes your hands to twitch as you touch it. The ominous skulls clearly indicate an evil-ish origin.

On the inside there is an inscribed message: Vis. Imperium. Regni Filum.

## Effects

Equipping the Gauntlets of Ogre Power grants the Cleric a +3 to their Strength.

## Trivia

The message inscribed in the gauntlets is Draconic (Latin) for "Power. Empire. String of the Kingdom."  C. Bodegård, Esoteric Ebb official Discord server. 02/03/2026 19:12 (requires Discord access to view).

The Gauntlets are worn by the Cleric in the art of the Strength and Wisdom focused Pre-built Primal Cleric.
