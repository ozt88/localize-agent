# Esuth

Esuth was an ancient dragon of historical importance. He was discussed most prominently in the book Folk Tales found in Visken's Lair.

## Background

Esuth was said to use trickery and betrayal to gain power.

He terrorized The Coast during the Gate Era, leaving swaths of it burned. This was to anger the titan Kha-Ymor and claim Kha-Ymor's domain for himself. Kha-Ymor succeeded in slaying Esuth with the help of a "half-wit" gnome Abacus.
