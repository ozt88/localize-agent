# The Undercommon

The Undercommon is an equippable piece of Literature. It can be found in the Tea Shop on a burnt bookshelf.

## In-game Description

A popular fan-zine. Shoddily printed and filled with letters, fanfiction, and reviews. It's essentially a collection of writing from various fans of local theatre shows, musical artists, and nerdy activities. This issue seems to be full of info about LARPING, which is taking the youth by storm.

The zine is infamous for accidentally releasing issues in an enchanted indestructible paper. A legal battle is being fought over the difficulty in removing them from circulation. You cannot destroy this magazine no matter what you do.
