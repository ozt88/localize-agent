# Abacus

Abacus was a gnome of legend of the Gate Era. He, along with the Mist Titan Kha-Ymor, was credited with slaying the dragon Esuth, as told in the book Folk Tales found in Visken's Lair.

He is described a a "halfwit" who met Kha-Ymor by accident and in the same way lead him to Esuth in Askan.
