# Mace of Terror

The Mace of Terror is a Weapon found in the Crypt, behind the Skeleton.

## In-game Description

Standard issue among the Urthguard. The soul of the previous user is clearly haunting it - the dude stares at you in the corner of your eye whenever you wield it. Best to not... use it in a fight.

## Effects

Equipping the Mace of Terror grants the Cleric +2 to their Constitution, +1 to their Wisdom, and -2 to their Strength.
