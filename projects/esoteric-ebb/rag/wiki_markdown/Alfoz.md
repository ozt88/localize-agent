# Alfoz

)

Alfoz B.E. is a dwarf and a leading figure in Tolstad's Azgalist movement. He is the Foreman of the Azgalist Worker's Party. He is the first character the player sees after first exiting Visken's Lair.

## Description

Alfoz is stubbornly dedicated to his position. Despite his rejection of boasting, he is a prideful person, and will react antagonistically to any doubt of his validity as a member of the Azgalists.

He similarly respects show of strength and will abide by those who prove themselves to him.

## Background

Alfoz was born on the 12th of Cronus, 489 AE, into the Bhan-Emvers family, an important clan for dwarves exiled to the surface. He was the fourth son born into the second branch of the family. As an Azgalist, he rejects the importance of the relation, as it undermines the egalitarianism of the ideology.
