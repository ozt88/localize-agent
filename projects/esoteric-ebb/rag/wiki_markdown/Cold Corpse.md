# Cold Corpse

Cold Corpse is an interactable corpse in the lower part of Visken's Lair. It starts the quest Three Hundred Pieces. It can be pilfered for 6 Crowns and 1 Garden Apple.

Intelligence identifies the corpse as an Askan human male in mid forties, a factory worker. He has been marked for "Potential Revivification". The corpse is protected from decay by the spell Gentle Repose, and deals damage to anyone who touches it.
