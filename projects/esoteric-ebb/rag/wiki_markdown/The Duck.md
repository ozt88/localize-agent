## Description

Duck is a silent, terse, but not wholly uncooperative character. Though he speaks in short, factual statements, he carries a sense of intensity and seriousness that he stoically doesn't break. He is not easily provoked, but will threaten and engage in certain circumstances.

He refers to The Cleric as a soldier, possibly hinting that The Duck is connected to the Urthguard or to The Cleric (see Behold Information). He will agree to teach The Cleric Inflict Wounds and to give advice.

## Background

Much of The Duck's identity remains a mystery. He divulges the information that he is there to guard the entrance to The City Below, into which he delves regularly.
