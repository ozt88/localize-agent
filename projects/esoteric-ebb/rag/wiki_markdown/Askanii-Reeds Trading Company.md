# Askanii-Reeds Trading Company

Askanii-Reeds Trading Company is a Freestrider-aligned business and organization, one of many throughout The Coast with multiple branches. In Tolstad their head-quarters are located in Waterlane.

Intelligence describes them as expansionist. Despite the name, their business ventures aren't described in detail in the demo. Instead Agent Viira states they are in Norvik to promote Freestriderism in Askan and "make sure election process goes smoothly".
