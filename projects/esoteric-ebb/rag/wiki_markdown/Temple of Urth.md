# Temple of Urth

Temple of Urth is a location in Tolstad North. It is a small religious site dedicated to the god Urth, now housing the upcoming elections and an entrance to The City Below.

## Description

The temple is located north of the Pillar of Jor, close to the center of Tolstad, up a staircase that eventually leads to upper region of the district. That district itself sits on top of the temple, as Norvik is a city built in layers. Since its god died, the temple is largely abandoned.

Urth's name is written above the entrance in Elder Futhark ᚢᚱᛏᚺ. In Runic scripts, the th-consonant is a single letter, ᚦ, but the game uses a digraph T+H, ᛏᚺ. On the official Discord server, the creator Christoffer Bodegård stated that this is because of the development of Dwarvish.

On each side of the entrance is a mural of the Great Perch, a legendary creature associated with Urth.

Inside, the temple resembles a small church, with a central shrine and wooden statue of Urth. The statue itself has been vandalized by Goblon. The Cleric can attempt to repair the statue using Dexterity at DC 12 . Having the Simple Hammer equipped will grant Advantage on this check.

On the stairs leading to the shrine sits The Duck, who acts as a roadblock to The City Below, entrance to which is locked, located in the north-east. In the north-western corner is hidden a Shard of Jor, and along the western wall lie ballot boxes waiting for the Election Week.
