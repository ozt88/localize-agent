# Constitution

Constitution (abbr. CON) is a character statistic, and a character in The Cleric's mind.

It dictates how resilient a character is.

## Mechanics

For more detailed information about abilities and dice rolls see Abilities.

Constitution's main specialty is higher hit points (i.e. health) per Ability Modifier. Though each level still increases hit points by 5, Constitution can increase the starting HP by 5 at maximum. Consequently, not investing in Constitution creates a Cleric that starts with only 3 HP.

Constitution is called for in dialogues for contests of resolve, defense, and to not succumb to ailments. It is often part of encounters.

## Personality

As a chime, Constitution is an odd character, arguably the most esoteric chime. It speaks in a cryptic way, even with direct statements on Cleric's health and ability.

As its visual representation (a stomach) suggest, its main interest is with eating. The Pre-built that focuses on Constitution, Beefy Cleric, has a quote of Constitution when trying to eat from the pile of apples at the start of the game: "Yes. More apples, Appleboy."

On the Voting Card, its political party corresponds to the Agrarians, a minor rural party subsumed by the Azgalian Worker's Party. However, in character creation it shows a blurb from a manifesto of the Hedonists, a political movement described as "anarchist" that dealt with "abyssal forces" (i.e. demonic). Though hedonism might just be a reference to a philosophy of maximizing pleasure, the group has similarities to the Nihilist movement in the Russian Empire, unrelated to the more popularly understood philosophical nihilism. The quote might have been used both to reference Constitution's endless hunger, and to characterize it as anarchistic force of self-interest.
