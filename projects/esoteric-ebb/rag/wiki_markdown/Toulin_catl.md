# Toulin'catl

The mollusca Toulin'catl were an ancient Askanii civilization of snailfolk. They disappeared during the Fade Era.

The Toulin'catl were masters of wizardry. One of the main factors that led to their eventual collapse was that they supported Jor in his war with the Gods. Their cities were burned to the ground by Esuth.

A number of ruins of structures made by the Toulin'catl can be found all around Tolstad.

The Journal definition of the Toulin'catl appears after succeeding a DC 18 Intelligence check and reads "Toulin'catl - an ancient Askanii civilization that disappeared during the Fade Era. Little is known of them, apart from their apparent mastery of magic, and their snail-like appearance." This is why a lot of the relics found across Tolstad have a snail motif.
