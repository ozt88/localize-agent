# Tinnitul

Tinnitul is a Smoke Mephit in the employ of the Askanii-Reeds Trading Company. He is bound to the fireplace of the Akanii-Reeds Trading Company Building in Waterlane.

Through conversation with Tinnitul, it is possible to learn that there is a hidden safe behind the mirror in the Trading Company building.

Tinnitul can be made to look the other way while you attempt to steal from the safe by convincing him you are a worthy elemental lord. This can be done by succeeding a DC 14 Constitution check to stick your hand in his fireplace. This check's DC will be reduce by 2 if you lowered yourself before Tinnitul while talking with him, and you will have advantage on it if you are capable of Druidcraft.
