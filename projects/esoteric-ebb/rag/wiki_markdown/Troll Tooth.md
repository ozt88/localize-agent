# Troll Tooth

The Troll Tooth is a Trinket left behind by Big Leader Gourgalash after he is defeated.

## In-game Description

The tooth of an Askanii troll. It shivers in your grip, trying to regenerate pieces of its marrow. Its esoteric properties have an effect on you.

## Effects

Equipping the Troll Tooth grants the Cleric a +1 to their Strength. It also grants "Quickly regenerate HP over time."

## Trivia

The Troll Tooth is worn by the Cleric in the art of the Wisdom and Strength focused Pre-built Primal Cleric.
