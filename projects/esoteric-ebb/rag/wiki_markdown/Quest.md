# Quest

Quests offer XP, rewards, and opportunities to explore and affect the world. Esoteric Ebb's main quest is The Tea Shop that Blew Up, but there are also dozens of side quests (some tracked, some not).

The Journal definition of a Quest appears after succeeding a DC 9 Strength check and reads: "Quest - a task performed by a heroic individual and/or professional adventurer."

There are three quest branches, each of which split off into smaller quests related to the topic of the branch quest. The three quest branches are The Tea Shop That Blew Up, Esoteric Ebb and Democratization.

## The Tea Shop that Blew Up

Dragon of Tolstad - You're here on request of Lady Sageleaf, a goblin chieftain in Tolstad. Go see her, right away. She's in the Goblin Garden, at the Southern Tip of Tolstad.

(Quest Unknown)

Coinlord's Meet - You do not have permission to enter the Tea Shop. You need authorization from the magistrate of Tolstad, Coinlord Darrow. You know his mansion can be found up the hill, on central Tolstad.

Valkyrie - Ettir, the angel working for Magistrate Darrow, is your roommate during this investigation. You find her elusive yet intriguing. Find out more by performing the most difficult of techniques: speak with her.

The Star Witness - A 'Frank son of Frank' was seen on Pillar Road at the time of the explosion, but calmly walked downstairs afterwards. Locate and question this strange man dressed in yellow. Frank son of Frank is often used as a fake name by rich adventurers around the Coast.

Gate of Jor - An invisible, 'smoking' man was seen entering the Pillar of Jor, right after the explosion. Now you need to figure out a way to follow. Seek the aid of some ancient powerful being that can help you.

Ruffled Feathers - The owner of the tea shop is unaccounted for. Locate this bird-man and find out what he knows.

Safe & Secure - Drummer has a safe hidden in his office, in the back of the tea shop.

Elven Connection - You found an Elven corpse under the tea shop rubble. It's been moved to the Lichhouse for safekeeping.

A Freelance Hag - There was a meeting planned in the tea shop on the day of the explosion - a meeting with a hag. Locate her, and find out what she knows.

Strings of Divination - Lisa wants you to prove your natural ability for divination magic. If you get her a 'maw pie' then she'll teach you a trick that could make it easier...

## Esoteric Ebb

Three Hundred Pieces - You encountered a corpse in the Lichhouse. It seems fresh and ready for revivify. Though it'd cost quite a bit of money to bring him back - almost a month's rent!

Philogynistic Agenda - Women. They're everywhere. Your worth as a man is based only on the admiration you get from them. Your goal? Get a date. A real date. Good luck.

Lost Mines of Toul'Amon - A dwarf named Akzel is looking for a legendary lost mine, somewhere in the tunnel to the southwest. But the HWBT won't allow him passage. Find a symbol of the sun, or a lack of 'the shrine', then report back to him to get a reward.

The Lost 6th Edition - Sch. Snurre Appledent has tasked you with retrieving a rare copy of Cividix's Book of the Mind, which is supposedly found somewhere in the dungeons of the City Below.

An Easy Riddle - The drunk sphinx in the Drunk Sphinx has offered you another riddle: if you solve it, you gain access to her vault. Are you up to the challenge?

Class Conflict - You're very opinionated. Now it's time to choose a path. Cleric. Rogue. Wizard. Or something else?

Primal Kin - Convince Nessan to teach you primal magic. If you're so inclined.

Phoenix Folio - After your river adventure, your old spellbook is ruined! Find a service provider who can rebind it for you. Fix your ruined spellbook, damn it!

Lichhouse Blues - The pale man called Visken is very suspicious. Find out what is going on in the Lichhouse.

River Take Ye - You were brought to the Lichouse by some fishermen. But how did you survive your plunge into the river?

Cult Hit - An assassin came for your life. You survived, but the attacker got away. With no clues to go on other than an esoteric mask, you must be ready for them to return at any time.

Crypt Keeper - There is a powerful spellcaster named Bondevol hiding down in the crypts. You must find him.

## Democratization

Election Week - The first ever election in Norvikian history will take place in just a few days' time. What will you vote for?

Missing Paladin - Lord Gorm of Norvik has gone missing. The legendary paladin is said to have disappeared far below the city...
Locate the missing Paladin. It will make you a hero.

Milkurious - Crown collection. Coin capture. Cash cidnapping. There are many ways to describe it. Get rich. Or die trying. The only way to do it is obviously to get this milk delivery gig. Try checking around waterlane in the morning...

Circle Square Conflict - An orc and a bunch of dwarves are in a deadlock. The dwarves want to hold a rally and the orc is sent to stop them.

Young and Dumb - You hear there's going to be trouble down on Waterlane this evening, on your first day on Tolstad. You should check it out.

Capital Dominion - Since you're a friend of the AR Trading Co., Viira offered you a gig: hand out AEFAs (Askanii Economic Freestrider Accord) to local entrepreneurs.

Copper Press Fever - An Azgalist newspaper is being printed on Tolstad. You should find the location of this copper press and the name of the secret editor.

Norvikian Resistance - You've heard of a secret society of 'pure' Nationalists who gather in the Rollermill. They must be very powerful.

Golden Line - There's a geopolitical conflict going on in the clanlands. The homeland of the Askanii-native halflings has been cut in half - by the so-called Golden Line. Celestials hold the west. Maybe you should find out more?

Hammer and Zeal - Rollo believes the information gathered from the Freestriders should be shared with the Azgals. He wants you to invite Modissa to a meeting.
