# Hit points

Hit Points (abbreviated to HP) is a representation of a character's health; i.e. points that decrease if taking damage (often also from social effects such as embarrassment), and which will trigger a Death Saving Throw when they run out.

Death Saving Throw is a flat roll (without modifiers) at DC 10. The roll keeps repeating until the player gets 3 successes or failures; 3 failures means the game ends, 3 successes leaves the player with 1 HP, but also increases Exhaustion (which lowers the maximum HP by 1). Getting a Critical Success instantly revives the Cleric without any Exhaustion, and a Critical Failure does the opposite.

There are several healing Consumables, Equippables, Spells, and special events (usually requiring passing an Ability Check). Rest also regains HP by rolling a number of Hit Dice (eight-sided) equal to the character's level (only 1 on Short Rest).

Maximum Hit Points is governed by character level (increases by 5 for each level), and adds the Constitution Ability Modifier.
