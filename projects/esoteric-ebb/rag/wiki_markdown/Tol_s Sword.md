# Tol's Sword

The Tol's Sword is a Weapon found after using the Deck of Many Things, and receiving permanent disadvantage on everything.

## In-game Description

A standard issue Urthguard blade. One handed usage, lightweight.

However, this blade is clearly inbued with esoteric energy. The feeling you get while touching it is so intense that it's impossible to wield it properly, at least not without getting used to it. There's also a strange engraving on the hilt that says:

We found the other side, brother. The gods never left. You must send help.
Wonder what that means?

## In-game Dialogue

Inside, you find a blade. It looks like any other. A regular... Urthguard Blade.

(Str DC 9 Success) The weapon which gave rise to the Standardized Urthguard Sword. This... is a DIVINE artifact. It is an honor to touch it. To stroke it tenderly.

What is this blade? Is it just a... +1 sword or something?"

No. (Int DC 9 Success) This intense esoteric aura is obviously something more. Perhaps +2. Or even +3.

(Str DC 15 Success) It is Tol's Sword. Urth's brother brought it out of the city, into the Endless Waste. The magic of the deck has brought it back to you. It is a LEGENDARY blade. Now it has ended up with you.

## Effects

Equipping the Tol's Sword grants the Cleric +4 to their Strength.
