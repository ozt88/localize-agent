# The Cleric

The Cleric is Esoteric Ebb's player character. His full name is Ragn Hemlin, though most of the narration in the game refers to him as The Cleric. His class and profession: the Arcane Cleric of the Urthguard.

## Description

As part of the Urthguard, The Cleric wears full armor complete with a helmet that covers his face. Strength insists to never lose his helmet, even though The Cleric can wear different helmets.

Though he is a scholar of magic, The Cleric's dialogue options mostly come off as unintelligent, regardless of Intelligence or Wisdom scores. As a player avatar, other characteristics are determined by the player. The official Dungeons & Dragons character sheet has a listed feature called "Askanii Barbarian", which might be a jab at his lack of forethought and all the possible simple (and Strength focused) solutions.

Despite his seeming incompetence, he has a deep connection with The Esoteric. The Abilities which provide in-game narration and guidance are said to be esoteric connections to magical forces. He also has an uncanny ability to quickly absorb spells. On occasions, The Cleric uses power of suggestion to perform better (or worse; changing DC in game terms), and possession of Items alone change his ability score, though equipment does not cause effects such as death when an ability reaches 0.

Sageleaf may describe him as having "eyes of a hawk", after succeeding a Wisdom check.

On the Voting Card it is shown that he has ticked every box. Pressed on this, he can say that this is because he is indecisive. The official D&D character sheet lists him as Nationalist, but also a "Democratic Herald".

## Background

Though it is up to player which background The Cleric should focus on, the information in them aren't mutually incompatible. In fact, all of the backgrounds are true and paint a chronological list of events within Ragn's life, reinforced by references to them in the game regardless of Background choice. It is also stated that Ragn is born on the turn of the Era, around the start of the Post-War Era (PE) on the date Chronus 13th, this means that Ragn is as old as the Post-War Era, thus making him 27 years old at the start of the game.

* In the official Dungeons & Dragons character sheet, Cleeric's Alignment is represented on two scales of 10 increments each. He has 3 points towards Good and 1 point towards Chaotic, making him a mostly Neutral Good character
** His other traits are that he will always dance when given the opportunity and is unable to turn down a quest.
* He still lives with his mother.
* His mother, Helga Hemlin, was a prominent human suffragette who wrote during the Norvikian suffragette movement.
