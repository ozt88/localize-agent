# Undercoast

Undersea Coast is a part of the Jor's Realm that is found underneath its surface.

Around 3-5 miles under sea level, gravity reverses. The same distance further is a cavernous surface with its own sea and an archipelago, the Dark Archipelago.

The Undercoast is a home to various ethnicities, such as the Underdwarves, Pimeä, and also a race of sentient mushrooms.
