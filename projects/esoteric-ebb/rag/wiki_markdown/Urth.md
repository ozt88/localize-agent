# Urth

.]]

Urth was the state god of Norvik, known by his epithet as the Father Of Norvik. He is The Cleric's god after whom the Urthguard are named, as well as the human ethnicity Urthfolk. He, like the other gods, died by the end of the Arcane Era.

One of the symbols of Urth is the drake-like sea creature Great Perch, as those, found in the River Torna, were tamed by him.

Urth had a brother, Tol. It is unclear if Tol also achieved divinity.

The Journal definition of Urth appears after succeeding a DC 1 Strength check and reads: "Urth - your god."
