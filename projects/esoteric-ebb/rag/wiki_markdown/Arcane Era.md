# Arcane Era

The Fourth Era. It lasted for 511 years, ending 27 years prior to the events of the game. It succeeded the Divine Era, and preceded the Postwar Era.

## Major Events

The Arcane Era was a time of great magical and technological growth. Powerful Human magocracies grew and fell to ruin in Diminor during this era. Arcanists took control over the entire Coast.

## Timeline

* 398 AE - Assassination of Princess Ilona, start of the Coast War
* 443 AE - Azgal Ashmask attains divinity and founds the Azgal Folkhem Forge
* 466 AE - Urthfolk's homeland becomes Curselands, beginning of The Conquest
* 468 AE - End of The Conquest, founding of Norvik after Urth defeats the Trollking 'Gourgalash' in the ruins of Toulin'catl.
* 470 AE - Azgal Ashmask dies, civil war causes his followers to be exiled to surface
* 481 AE (14th of Chronus) - Fall of Thazen, end of the Coast War
* 483 AE - Goblin War begins
* 488 AE - Goblin War ends
* 489 AE - Crann signs the Goblin Treaty, the three goblin clans join Norvik
* 499 AE - Declaration of the Urthfolk Rights Order, which ended alignment discrimination in the Norvikian courts.
* 511 AE - End of the Arcane Era
