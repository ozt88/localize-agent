# Norvikian Resistance

Norvikian Resistance is a major quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of Young and Dumb and branches into Hammer and Zeal.

.

## Quest Record

You've heard of a secret society of 'pure' Nationalists who gather in the Rollermill. They must be very powerful.

Completing Norvikian Resistance will allow you to choose from one of three feats related to the quest.

Defend the City - Alters ability score modifiers on HELMETS. Positive modifiers are increased by 1 and negative modifiers are decreased by 1.

True Norvikian - Roll initiative with Strength, if it is higher than your Dexterity.

Leviathan Apparatus - Enemies always roll initiative with disadvantage.
