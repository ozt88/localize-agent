# Snurre Appledent

Snurre Appledent is a halfling scholar in Waterlane. He is a Scholar of the Fordnip University, specializing in the field of AIS(Alignment Intent Studies).

## Description

Snurre defines himself as a "Filthy Azgalist". He believes that the only way to achieve a truly classless society is through the upheaval of the current form of government. He thinks that the Azgalian Worker's Party has deviated from true Azgalism, and instead wishes to compromise with the ruling class. Despite this, he votes for the Worker's Party, as he believes that it is better than nothing."
