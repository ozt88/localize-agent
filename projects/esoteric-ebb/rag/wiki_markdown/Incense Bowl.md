# Incense Bowl

The Incense Bowl is a Trinket found in the City Below the City.

## In-game Description

A chipped stone bowl. It contains several layers of ancient incense. If kept on your person, it grants a passive incense burn.

It also makes you smell a bit wonky, of course. And the smoke is really bad for your lungs. But at least it's not addicting.

## Effects

Equipping the Incense Bowl grants the Cleric +1 to their Intelligence and Wisdom, and -1 to their Constitution and Charisma. Additionally, it recovers a 1st level spell slot every 3 hours, similar to burning Incense.
