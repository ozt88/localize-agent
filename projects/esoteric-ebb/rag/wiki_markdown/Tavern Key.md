# Tavern Key

They Tavern Key is an equipable Key Item given by Olzis in the Drunk Sphinx during dialogue.

The Tavern Key is required to enter the Drunk Sphinx before 6 PM on any day.

## In-game Description

A very pretty key. It's esoterically attuned to the Sphinx's Lair - i.e. the local tavern. This will allow you to enter during the day-time, so you can solve her riddle! Or just visit.

## Effect

Equipping the Tavern Key grants the Cleric +1 to their Charisma. Owning the key also lets the Cleric enter the Drunk Sphinx before 6 PM on any day.
