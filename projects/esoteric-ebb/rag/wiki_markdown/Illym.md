# Illym

Illym The Mender is an Elven goddess who established the Illym Empire. The Illym Forest is also named after her. She died at the end of the Arcane Era, decades before Urth.

She is said to have been stunningly beautiful and a terrifying witch. Supposedly, when she invaded Norvik, she "charmed a hundred warriors". Urth brokered peace with her, but after her death, the empire became expansionist.

Her descendants add the title Athena to their names, e.g. Pínja'Athena.
