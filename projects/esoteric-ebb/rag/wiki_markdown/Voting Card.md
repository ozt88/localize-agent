# Voting Card

The Voting Card is a Literature item. It can be found in the lower layer of Visken's Lair, next to the staircase leading up.

## In-game Description

A scuffed voting card made out to 'Ragnar Hemlin.' It's from a dwarven office, for some reason, and someone has checked in all five parties on the list.

You need to bring this to a polling place on election day if your voice is to be heard.

## Effects

Equipping the Voting Card causes the Cleric to get a -1 debuff to their Intelligence.
