# Tiny Dictator

The Tiny Dictator is a Trinket found in Snurre's Office.

## In-game Description

An unseen servant magically attuned to a feather pencil and paper - it will write down everything spoken within 10 feet. The content disappears off the page as it writes. The servant can follow commands to write down specific topics, keep the notes in a certain style, or focus on a chosen person's words.

These are very common in the halls of the Palace, where words can ruin lives—or worse: careers.

## Effects

Equipping the Tiny Dictator grants the Cleric a +2 to their Intelligence, and gives them advantage against Bureaucrats. Characters that count as bureaucrats are:
* Agent Viira
* Haelon Bondavol
* Ambassador Icvx Liuvben
