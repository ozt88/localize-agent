# Azgal Ashmask

Azgal Ashmask was a low caste dwarf born in the tunnels of Lekm-Muraz. In 443 AE, he became the God of exiles and egalitarianism after starting a workers' revolution. His teachings established one of the major political ideologies of Azgalism, whose adherents tend to reject the idea that Azgal became a god.

He opposed gods and kingdoms, seeking to establish an egalitarian society. He founded the Azgal Folkhem Forge, a state located in the Undercoast.

Like the other gods, he died during the previous era, 470 AE. After his death, a war broke out, which resulted in his followers being banished to the surface.

The Journal definition of Azgal Ashmask appears after succeeding a DC 12 Wisdom check and reads: "Azgal Ashmask - a dwarf of a low caste born deep in the Lekm-Muraz tunnels. When he attained divinity in 443 AE, he crushed the kingdoms and formed the Azgal Folkhem Forge. On his death in 470 AE, a great civil war broke out, resulting in the exiling of all his followers to the surface. But his influence was felt all around the Undercoast and above, as his Exiled dwarves spread Azgalism to all corners of Jor."
