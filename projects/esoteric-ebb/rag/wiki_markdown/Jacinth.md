# Jacinth

The Jacinth is a Trinket obtained after solving the 3 riddles of a Minotaur statue in the City Below the City.

The Jacinth can be inserted into the Whitemane Shrine in the South Caverns.

## In-game Description

A red gemstone. It's very pretty, and probably very expensive. Once this whole election business is over, you could either sell it on the black market or turn it in at the Tower for a reward. Neither wouldn't really net you a fortune, but maybe a fraction of a fortune?

## Effects

Equipping the Jacith grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength and -1 to their Intelligence.
