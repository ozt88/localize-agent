# Askanii

Askanii (sometimes Askan) is one of the eight bands along The Coast. It neighbors Reeds and Divinii Torum.

Askanii is home to many nations, such as the city-state of Norvik, the Vyz-Kha Kingdoms, and the Fordnip Commonwealth.

Askanii's landscape is mountainous. The River Torna runs through the band, dividing it into two.

### Askanii Seasons

* Askanii Spring - 3rd of Beltan
* Askanii Summer - 28th of Chronus
* Askanii Autumn - 14th of Eltre
* Askanii Winter - 7th of Soumil
