# Thazen

Thazen was the greatest of the floating cities. During the Coast War, it was used as a center of operations by the Haagin-Dim Coalition. It had long shadowy tentacles that could extend up to a thousand feet from the city.

Thazen fell to the ground on the 14th of Chronus in the year 481 AE. It was later rebuilt as a non-floating city.
