# Lady Fair

Lady Fair was a god, and an ascended halfling.

The Journal description of Lady Fair appears after succeeding a DC 14 Wisdom check and reads: "Lady Fair and Those Not - a young halfling leads her clan through a mountain pass and defends it for three days and three nights. She is responsible for the founding of the Fordnip Commonwealth."

## Description

Like other gods, Lady Fair was once a mortal. She was a young halfling who, unlike most gods, only possessed the divine spark for a few days. She led her clan of five thousand Halflings through snowy mountains and defended them for three days and three nights.

She was the founder of the Fordnip Commonwealth.

## Trivia

The Feat Lady Fair is named after her.
