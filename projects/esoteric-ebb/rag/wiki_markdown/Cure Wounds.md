# Cure Wounds

Cure Wounds is a basic healing spell. It is the earliest and easiest spell to acquire, found sticking out of the pile of apples in Visken's Lair where The Cleric first wakes up.

## Effect

A creature you touch regains a number of hit points equal to 1d8 + your Wisdom ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Can be cast on self.

## Trivia

* As of the demo, this is the only spell that has three different schools stated (The Cleric uses it as an Evocation). This is in reference to different editions of Dungeons & Dragons, where it changed from being a Necromancy spell to a Conjuration spell in Third Edition, to an Evocation spell in Fifth Edition, which the game is based on. It is not yet known why the schools in the world of The Coast are Evocation, Necromancy and Abjuration.
