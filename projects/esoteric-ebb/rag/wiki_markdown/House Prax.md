# House Prax

House Prax is a magocractic family. The last living member of the family is Rubrick Prax, the head of The Arcane Circle, an Arcanist organization.

Filius Prax was an early historical figure and author of Autobiography Prax, quote of which appears during Character Creation.

They are notable for creating the Curselands. Their other notable specialties are golems and esoteric architecture.
