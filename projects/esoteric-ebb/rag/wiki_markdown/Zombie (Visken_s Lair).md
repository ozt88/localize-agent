# Zombie (Visken's Lair)

In the lower half of Visken's Lair on day 1, a zombie is interacting with the pile of apples.

This zombie acts as a roadblock to the northern half of the lower lair. It can be engaged in combat, which leads to few possible outcomes that let The Cleric pass through. The zombie disappears once Snell joins the party.

The zombie has the item Wisdom Tooth, which can be looted if the zombie gets knocked out, or pickpocketed at DC 15. The zombie and the tooth or clues for the quest Lichhouse Blues.
