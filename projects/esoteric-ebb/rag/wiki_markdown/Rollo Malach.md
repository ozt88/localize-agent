# Rollo Malach

Stenbusken is the leader of the Norvikian Resistance. He can usually be found in the Rollermill or Tavern with his friends.

## Description

Rollo is a reasonably tall and muscular teenager, with blond hair and dark blue eyes.

## Background

Rollo is somewhat hostile to Modissa due to her being a member of the Azgalian Worker's Party. It can be revealed in conversation with Rollo and the rest of the Norvikian Resistance that Rollo had a crush on Modissa when they were still in school. Although being a bit hostile towards her, he does not hate her outright, and even suggests seeking her help in the quest Hammer and Zeal.

Rollo's father died while using the toilet, and he can be revived by the Cleric as part of the quest Three Hundred Pieces. Discussing the topic of his father with Rollo will reveal that he and his brother have a strained relationship with their father due to him being a poor, oftentimes abusive, parent.
