# Azgalian Worker's Party

Azgalian Worker's Party are a political party in Norvik, part of the Azgalist movement. They are labor-oriented and base their ideology in the ideas of its founder Azgal Ashmask.

## Background

Azgal Ashmask was a low caste dwarf born in the tunnels of Lekm-Muraz, who led a revolution and established the Azgal Folkhem Forge state located in the Undercoast. Some say he was a dwarven god of exiles and egalitarianism, but as Azgalism's core tenets deny the rule of gods, this is disputed by Azgalists.

After his "untimely fading", the ideology spread to The Coast. In Norvik, this spread is headed by the Norvik Azgal Council.

## Description

Azgalism predominantly targets workers and marginalized minorities to propagate the ideology. It is heavily associated with dwarves, though their main concern is workers. They start and manage labor unions and propose the idea of common folk being in charge of governance.

They oppose Freestriders, being an anti-capitalist movement. They also oppose gods and rule of kings or mages (called magocracy).

They have established control over several regions: the Azgalian Undercoast State, Vyz-Kha kingdoms, and the Three Wings of Divinii Torum. However, all of these states have collapsed.

They self-print and illegally propagate a manifesto called The Azgal Chant.

The extreme wing of the Norvikian party is called the Azgalian Front.

## Trivia

* Their party color is green.
* The party's logo is a green hammer.
* The Cleric used to be involved in propagating the movement.
* The Agrarian Rural Party was reported by The Norvikian Post to subsume into the Azgalists when loosing most of the working class vote. Agrarians are the party of farmers in Askan. Wisdom claims that the Agrarians joined the Azgalists because the shareholders of farms refused to increase wages of the farmhands and subsequently fired them, which lead to a further radicalization. This also almost resulted in a famine.
