# Frog-Mouth

The Frog-Mouth is a Helmet found atop a dresser in Darrow's Nest.

Talking to the Nicholas Darrow while wearing the helmet will cause him to comment on it, with him stating that the The Cleric is free to wear it if they so please.

## In-game Description

A helmet specifically made for various forms of jousting, with limited usage outside such cases. It looks fancy, however.

## Trivia

The Frog-Mouth is worn by the Cleric in the art of the Charisma focused Pre-built Unstable Cleric.
