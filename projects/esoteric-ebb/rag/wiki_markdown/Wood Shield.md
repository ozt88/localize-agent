# Wood Shield

The Wood Shield is a Trinket that belongs to Moongore. It can be obtained by pickpocketing her.

## In-game Description

A battle-hardened piece of brachen. The fact that this esoterically durable piece of wood has cracks in it is... impressive, to say the least. It is adorned with the symbol of the HWBT, and most likely procured personally by Lady Sageleaf for her top guardians.

## Effects

Equipping the Wood Shield grants the Cleric a +2 to their Strength and a -1 to their Charisma.
