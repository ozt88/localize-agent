# The Coast

The Esoteric Coast (also known as Realm of Jor, Jor's Realm, or JOR) is the name of the dimension created by the wizard Jor. It is the world in which Esoteric Ebb takes place.

The land is surrounding an ocean, forming a ring magically divided into eight bands, each with its own unique traits, and with its own Pillar of Jor. Surrounding them is the  Infinite Wastes — esoteric, unexplorable areas. Underneath The Coast, is the Undersea Coast (Undercoast for short), a cavern and a vastly numerous archipelago with reversed gravity.

## The bands

### Askanii

Askanii (sometimes referred to as Askan) is the band which contains Tolstad, where the game takes place. It is a cold and mountainous region.

### Reeds

Reeds (formerly known as (Old) Diminor) is the homeland of humans. It has a temperate climate and is mostly lowlands, with notable amount of lakes. It has a large presence of the undead due to a curse. It was renamed after losing in the Coast War, which left the land heavily damaged.

### Vourgeni

Vourgeni is a highland defined by gravity-defying mountains. Its inhabitants are giants and some humans and gnomes.

### Hae'Xi

Hae'Xi is described as a "hellscape", due to its volcanic rivers and cursed jungles. It is home to abyssal creatures such as demons and tieflings.

### Kargamesh

Kargamesh is a biome of desert and shrubland, mostly occupied by halflings, elementals, and planetouched. It's settlements surround oases or are dug underground.

### Fell Tire

Fell Tire has red soil that has been made magically fertile in lowlands. It is home to Orcs, Bloodcity Bay being the most notable city.

### Ym

Ym has artificial forests and crags. Places in the band are covered in eternal darkness. It is a homeland of Elves, who are at war with devils and celestials.

### Divinii Torum

The whole land is a slope leading to the Sacred Wasteland, inhabited by celestials and halflings. Its name is Latin for "divine of elevation".

## History

For more details, read Eras.

Approximately more than 5,000 years before the events of the game, a magician Jor constructed his own plane of existence and inhabited it with giants and other large creatures such as dragons who shaped the lands, along with his magic. When he opened the gates to his realm, it was invaded by the Gods of the original dimension who fought him and the Beasts of Jor for domination over the realm.

Many other creatures entered and settled in The Coast, but due to the war Jor closed the gates and disappeared, trapping them permanently. After a period of 1111 years, the traces of the original gods awakened in mortals, creating a new pantheon who controlled the realm until mages established an autocratic rule. These were abolished with the Coast War.
