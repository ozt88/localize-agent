# Hae'Xi

Hae'Xi is one of the eight bands that makes up The Coast. Its name means "Growing Maw" or "Into the Air" in abyssal. It neighbors Kargamesh and Vourgeni.

Hae'Xi is covered in dense jungle. It is filled with countless hazards, such as flesheating insects and rivers of lava.

The Journal entry for Hae'Xi appears after succeeding a DC 12 Intelligence check and reads "Hae'Xi - one of the bands along the Coast. Means either "Growing Maw" or "Into the Air" in abyssal. A deeply cursed jungle hellscape filled with volcanic rivers, flesheating insects, and murderous plants. Once an elven sanctuary, now home to demonic warlords, tiefling cities, and celestial crusaders. "

## History

Hae'Xi was once the home of a large number of Elves. At some point it was taken over by external forces, and now acts as a home to Demons, Tieflings, and Celestials.
