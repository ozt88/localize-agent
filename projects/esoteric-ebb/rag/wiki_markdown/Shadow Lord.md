# Shadow Lord

A walking god who lived early in the Divine Era. He was most well known for his legion of Death Knights, and for their almost century long reign over Old Diminor.

The Journal definition for the Shadow Lord appears after succeeding on a DC 11 Wisdom check and reads: "Shadow Lord - an early walking god of the Divine Era. Known for his legion of Death Knights, and the grasp they held over Old Diminor for close to a century. His city, Savalni, still stands today, and is a center for his remnant Shadow Clerics."

The Shadow Lord was the founder of the city Savalni. Even after his death, the city is a center for his remnant Shadow Clerics.

Some of the Shadow Lord's descendants made up the magocratic family House Hagginshags during the Arcane Era.
