# Party Of Norvik

Party Of Norvik (also known as The Nationalists) are a Norvikian political party and the current ruling party of the city.

## Background

In the game, Norvik is five days away from the first ever democratic elections. The Coast is in what's called a Postwar Era, 27 years after the Coast War. Previous era, the Arcane Era, was marked by an autocratic rule of mages called magocracy.

Norvik is currently divided into districts that each have their own administrator called a magistrate. The highest office is hold by a sole ruler called Lord Patriarch, currently held by Lord Patriarch Âkesam. Patriarch is also a clerical position. The city's principal god is Urth.

## Description

The party functions as the status quo, and an early political right.

Their common name, The Nationalists, implies a connection to the real world nationalism, though most of their features are theocratic and possibly monarchist. Their ideology in game is referred to as numenism.

Their leader is the current Lord Patriarch Âkesam, said to be appointed by the god Urth. He is criticized as being withdrawn, relegating his power to the magistrates. The other challenge the party faces is that Urth is now dead.

The party owns the newspaper The Norvikian Post, which is also the most popular newspaper. In the polls conducted by the newspaper, the party is in the lead at 40% of voters. With Charisma of 10, the ability chimes in to say the polls are done in rich, human areas, implying they are inaccurate, and also implying the party favors Humans and is racist.

## Trivia

* Their party color is red.
* Their symbol is an eye, which is also the symbol of Urth.
* Strength speaks in support of the party, as well as being loyal to Urth.
