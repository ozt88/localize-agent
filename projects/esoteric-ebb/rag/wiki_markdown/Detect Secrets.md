# Detect Secrets

Detect Secrets is a utility spell. It can be extracted from the Pickaxe of Secrets with a DC 14 Intelligence check. It takes 3 in-game hours to extract.

## Effect

If there is a trap, hidden cache, or secret door that you are not aware of within range, you create a translucent trail guiding you to its location.

### List of Detectable Secrets

* 2x Bonedust beneath a loose tile in the Drunk Sphinx
* 90x Crowns beneath a loose brick in Pillar Crossing
* Vourgeni River Droplets beneath a stone in Waterlane
* Pavise buried under a garden in Tolstad East
* Entrance to Secret Tunnel in Temple of Urth
* Spent Ring of Spell Storing behind loose bricks in Temple of Urth
* Revivify scroll and Dreampatch behind illusory wall in Visken's Lair
* Hidden staircase in the Crypt
* Illusory wall in Roots
* 110x Crowns and Pixie Dust Slush in a drawer in Darrow's Nest
* Holy Symbol of Whitemane in statue's maw in the Survey
