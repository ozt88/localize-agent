# Miska Sageleaf

Miska Sageleaf (often referred to as Lady Sageleaf) is the leader of the Halfway Bridge Tribe and administrator of Lower Tolstad.

She is the main quest giver who summoned The Cleric to investigate The Tea Shop That Blew Up. She resides on a tree throne in the Goblin Gardens, fist accessed through an entrance in the south-east of Tolstad South after talking to Snell.

## Description

Lady Sageleaf is an imposing figure that has a magical aura that requires Constitution saving throws (on a failure, Cleric takes damage). Goblins of Tolstad follow her with great reverence bordering on worship. Her demeanor demands respect and she will go on the offensive at any sign of disrespect. As harsh as she or her threats can be, she is also shown to not be cruel without necessity to protect her subjects.

Visken describes her the "de facto law-keeper" of Tolstad. The extents of her area of administration as well as her power are contested by the administration, though she is recognized officially. With the rising political tensions and changing landscape of Norvik, she seeks to protect the interests of HWBT, though she declines to support any given party as to not skew the opinion of her followers. She is however antagonistic towards the current administration.

Despite her high social standing, her clothes are tattered and worn. Her throne being made of wood and her shoulder pads out of leaves implies a connection to nature, which would explain her disheveled robe.

## Background

With several hints and high Wisdom Behold check, the player can find out how Sageleaf got so powerful.

The Sageleafs have been part of HWBT for five goblin generations (around 50 years). Sageleaf claims to have united the three goblin tribes, though it is uncertain as to when that was.
