# Lake Cujah

A salt water lake located in Hae'Xi. It is connected with a similar body of water in the Undercoast, and is the only body of water aside from Jor's Sea to pass the Gravity Flip.

The Journal entry for Lake Cujah appears after succeeding a DC 14 Intelligence check and reads "Lake Cujah - a salt water lake found in the jungles of Hae'Xi. Connected with a similar lake found in the Undercoast. Many argue it should be considered one body of water, the only of its kind to pass the Gravity Flip aside from Jor's Sea itself."
