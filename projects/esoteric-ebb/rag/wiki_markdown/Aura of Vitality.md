# Aura of Vitality

Aura of Vitality is a 3rd-level, Primal spell.

## Effect

Healing energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. During an encounter, each round you and all allies in the aura regain 2d6 hit points. Outside an encounter, you gain 20d6 hit points.
