# Jor

Jor was a powerful wizard who created the dimension of The Coast, which is why it's also called Jor's Realm or simply JOR.

As the creator of the realm, the magic in the world had been woven by him and is referred to as Jor's Strings.

Jor created The Coast approximately more than 5,000 years before the events of the game. At first, he opened portals to the realm for inhabitants of other planes to enter and populate it; hence why the time was given the name Gate Era. Towards the end of the Gate Era, Gods of the other realms sought to take power over the land, resulting in wars and eventual closing of the gates by Jor and starting the Fade Era.

As of the demo, it is uncertain what exactly happened to Jor afterwards. One entry says he went "silent", another that he died during The Closing. His magic persists similarly to the divine essence of the original gods. In the beginning of Esoteric Ebb, the introductory dialogue in which The Cleric receives questions is addressed from Jor.
