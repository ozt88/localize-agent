# Pickaxe of Secrets

The Pickaxe of Secrets is a Weapon obtained by talking to Akzel Mâddson after completing the quest Lost Mines of Toul'Amon. It is an ordinary pickaxe which has been enchanted with the spell Detect Secret.

While examining the Pickaxe, a DC 16 passive Intelligence check can reveal that the pickaxe's nonfunctionality is caused by one of the runes enchanting it having been sliced in half. After speaking the keyword ('secret'), a DC 14 passive Wisdom check can reveal that the pickaxe is not pointing up, but in fact pointing towards the Pillar of Jor.

A DC 14 Intelligence check lets the player figure out how to extract the 'Detect Secret' spell from the pickaxe. This check's DC will be reduced by 6 if you noticed that one of the runes was sliced, and by a further 4 if you received instruction on spellcraft from Visken. It will be made with advantage if it was noticed that the pickaxe is pointing towards the Pillar.

Extracting the spell takes 3 in-game hours.

## In-game Description

A highly dysfunctional pickaxe. It's supposed to point towards the nearest 'secret'. A trap, illusionary wall, or revealing other hidden dungeoneering goodies. However, it just keeps trying to point upwards whenever you try to wield it, making it impossible to actually use.

Though the runes look quite interesting...

## Effects

Equipping the Pickaxe of Secrets grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength and Intelligence.
