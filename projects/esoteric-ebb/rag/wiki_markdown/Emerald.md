# Emerald

The Emerald is a Trinket pickpocketed from Haelon Bondavol at DC 15. Alternatively, you can convince him to give it to you by mentioning Olzis wants it in order to make a gate key.

## In-game Description

A green gemstone. It's very pretty, and probably very expensive. Once this whole election business is over, you could either sell it on the black market or turn it in at the Tower for a reward. Neither wouldn't really net you a fortune, but maybe a fraction of a fortune?

(You also have a feeling this one holds some esoteric potential...)

## Effects

Equipping the Emerald grants the Cleric +1 to their Wisdom and -1 to their Intelligence.
