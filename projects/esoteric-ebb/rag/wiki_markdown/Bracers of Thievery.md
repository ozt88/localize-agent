# Bracers of Thievery

The Bracers of Thievery are a Trinket found in Visken's Lair on a stone platform after the Burned Corpse is brought to it.

## In-game Description

A pair of iron-and-leather bracers, marked by symbols of Illym craftsmanship. They are extremely magical, and grants the wearer an intense improvement to sleight of hand and trifling-related activities.

## Effects

Equipping the Bracers of Thievery grants the Cleric a +2 to their Dexterity, and lowers the DC of Pickpocketing checks by their proficiency modifier.

## Trivia

The Bracers are worn by the Cleric in the art of the Dexterity focused Pre-built Trickster Cleric.
