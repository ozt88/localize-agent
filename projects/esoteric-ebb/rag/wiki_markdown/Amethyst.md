# Amethyst

The Amethyst is a Trinket given by the Hail Snail after freeing him.

## In-game Description

A green gemstone. It's very pretty, and probably very expensive. Once this whole election business is over, you could either sell it on the black market or turn it in at the Tower for a reward. Neither wouldn't really net you a fortune, but maybe a fraction of a fortune?

## Effects

Equipping the Amethyst grants the Cleric +1 to their Charisma and -1 to their Intelligence.

## Trivia

Despite the fact that the Amethyst is obviously purple, its description states that it is green. This is most likely because its description was copied from that of the Emerald.
