# Crusader's Mantle

Crusader's Mantle is an Evocation spell. It can be found in the City Below, buried under some rocks that can be noticed by Detect Secret.

## Effect

Holy Power radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius, awakening boldness in you and all other friendly creatures. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. While in an encounter, you and all allies add 1d12 to ability checks.
