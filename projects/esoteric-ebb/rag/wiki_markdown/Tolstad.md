# Tolstad

Tolstad is the central district of Norvik, and the setting of the game. It is an island fortress on River Torna.

## Description

The only connection Tolstad has to the other four districts is through "many bridges". Tolstad is built on layers of past civilizations, new buildings built on ancient fortresses. The newest portions, furthest from the ground, are located in the north-east, while south-west still has vegetation.

Large portion of it is underground in a network of tunnels, living spaces largely situated in the central Pillar's Crossing, surrounding the great Pillar of Jor. At the river's water level, in the underground center, is Waterlane, the largest trade hub of Askan, built by dwarves. It is considered one of the "wonders of the world".

A great tree rises from the Goblin Garden in the underground, penetrating the top layer near the center.

Tolstad is full of air shafts that circulate air, both cooled and heated, likely magical, though most do not know its mechanisms.
