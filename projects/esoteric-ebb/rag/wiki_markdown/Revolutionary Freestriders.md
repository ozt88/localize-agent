# Revolutionary Freestriders

Revolutionary Freestriders are a Norvikian political party and a wider ideology promoting electoral democracy and laissez-faire capitalism.

## Background

The Coast War saw the Realm of Jor change from an autocratic rule of mages and gods, called magocracy, to democracy. The Freestriders were the main faction which spurred this revolutionary war to end magocracy.

In the East, Freestriders took control over several cities (their core situated in Reeds), referred to as Free City Alliance.

## Description

Freestriders create companies that trade across The Coast as well as participate in elections as a party promoting capitalist interests. They control many enterprises, spurning the ire of Azgalists.

Wisdom, a chime aligned with Azgalian Worker's Party, criticizes them for pushing propaganda and buying votes.

## History

The Freestrider movement originated in Old Diminor, towards the end of the Coast War.
