# Wisdom

Wisdom (abbr. WIS) is a character statistic, and a character in The Cleric's mind.

It is an intuitive understanding and awareness.

## Mechanics

For more detailed information about abilities and dice rolls see Abilities.

Compared to Intelligence, Wisdom is harder to define in concrete examples. Esoteric Ebb defines Wisdom as empathy, intuition, extra-sensory knowledge, perceptiveness, and a connection to esoteric and natural, primal forces.

Wisdom is important for Cleric's spellcasting, as the class cleric uses Wisdom for its spellcasting modifier.

## Personality

As a chime, Wisdom is an archetype of a bleeding heart, concerned with sympathy for others, especially the oppressed. Wisdom can sometimes appear as overly fussy, and it uses more poetic phrasing in certain situations.

The game links Wisdom with femininity. The writer Christoffer Bodegård  says in an interview with the YouTube channel The 41st Precinct: "Esoteric Ebb is at its core about, essentially, masculinity[...]" The game could be thematically seen as a struggle between the masculine Strength and the feminine Wisdom, though most Abilities are antagonistic to one another, to Wisdom especially Dexterity and Intelligence.

Wisdom is proponent of Azgalism, an equivalent to socialist or other labour ideologies.
