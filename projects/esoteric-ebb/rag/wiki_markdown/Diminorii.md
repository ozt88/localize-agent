# Diminorii

The Diminorii are one of the most powerful magocratic families in Old Diminor. They were the descendants of Doug Doulin Dim, the Drake Slayer King.

The Diminorii were well known for their reality-warping artifacts and enchantments.

Before the Coast War, they created the Haagin-Dim Coalition with House Hagginshags, "arguably" creating the most powerful Arcanist state. This merger is considered as part of what initiated the war.
