# Lady Chamar

Lady Chamăr is a human who came to Tolstad from the Holy Mountain. She doesn't play any major role in the demo, and her purpose is enigmatic. She isn't present in the morning, but appears west of the entrance to Temple of Urth, North of the center of Tolstad.

## Background

Chamăr is a servant of the Golden Horde from Divinii Torum, place belonging to Celestials. She says she is "in service of the ambassador", probably connected to Ettir.
