# Dragon of Tolstad

Dragon of Tolstad is a minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of the The Tea Shop that Blew Up.

## Walkthrough

## Quest Record

You're here on request of Lady Sagelief, a goblin chieftain in Tolstad. Go see her, right away. She's in the Goblin Garden, at the Southern Tip of Tolstad.
