# House Hagginshags

House Hagginshags was a powerful magocratic family in Old Diminor. They were the descendants of the Shadow Lord.

They were well known for their potions and necromancy.

Before the Coast War, they created the Haagin-Dim Coalition with House Diminorii, "arguably" creating the most powerful Arcanist state. This merger is considered as part of what initiated the war.
