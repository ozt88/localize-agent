# Stenbusken

Stenbusken is a member of the Norvikian Resistance. He can usually be found in the Rollermill or Tavern with his friends.

## Description

Stenbusken appears to be a relatively tall and muscular boy, although he is not very intelligent.

## Background

Although Stenbusken is considered to be unemployed, he states that he sometimes works in Waterlane, likely doing manual labor.
