# Strange Mushroom Juice

Strange Mushroom Juice is a consumable that increases the Cleric's Wisdom by +1 for a week. Two bottles of Strange Mushroom Juice can be found across Tolstad: one can be taken from a nature spirit beneath the Imposing Tree, provided the player has Druidcraft, and one can be bought from the Potion Seller for 260 Crowns.

## In-game description

Fermented mushrooms, often coven-produced. Must be decades old - the lumps seem to move around slightly.

## Effect

Consuming Strange Mushroom Juice causes the Cleric's Wisdom to increase by +1 for a week.
