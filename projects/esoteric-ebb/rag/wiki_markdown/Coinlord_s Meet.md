# Coinlord's Meet

Coinlord's Meet is a minor quest in Esoteric Ebb.

It branches off of the The Tea Shop that Blew Up.

## Quest Record

You do not have permission to enter the Tea Shop. You need authorization from the magistrate of Tolstad, Coinlord Darrow. You know his mansion can be found up the hill, on central Tolstad.
