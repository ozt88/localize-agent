# Gods

Walking gods (or demi-gods) of The Coast are ascended mortals who carried divinity of the original gods residing outside of The Coast.

## History

Realm of Jor was created by a magician, Jor. During its first era, the realm was opened to other realms, and towards the end of the era original gods invaded it to assert their power there.

When Jor closed the gates to his realm, the divine essence of the gods lingered. In the third era, called the Divine Era for this reason, this essence awakened divine avatars in mortals. Though not in origin the same as the "True Gods", these became the de facto gods of The Coast.

They were the major powers during the Divine Era, and continued to be worshiped in the following Arcane Era, when the realm was ruled by magocrats. Towards the end of AE, a war broke out, which ended magocratic hegemony over the Coast. Though not explicitly stated when and how, all known gods died during the course of the Arcane Era, possibly due to the war. Despite this, their divine power still lingers, allowing clerics to still utilize divine magic.
