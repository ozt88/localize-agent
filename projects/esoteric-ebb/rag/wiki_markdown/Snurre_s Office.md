# Snurre's Office

Snurre's Office is a location in Waterlane. It is an office owned by Snurre Appledent, who can be found within it.

## Description

Snurre's Office is located in the east of Waterlane. It is located immediately below the Temple of Urth, as the entrance to the Crypt within the Temple passes immediately above the office.

Snurre Appledent can be found sleeping within the office.

The spell Detect Alignment can be learned by reading 'The Dark Book of Shadow', located on the bookcase in the left of the office.
