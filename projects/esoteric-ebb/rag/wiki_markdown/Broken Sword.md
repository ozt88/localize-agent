# Broken Sword

The Broken Sword is a Weapon found in the lower part of Visken's Lair, north-west of the room, on a barrel, after dealing with the Zombie (Visken's Lair).

## In-game Description

A standard issue Urthguard blade. One handed usage, lightweight. Crap quality. Makes you feel like a big man. An agent of the state.

It's also broken in half. But if you hide lean tactically, you can hide that fact from your enemies.

## Effects

Equipping the Broken Sword grants the Cleric +1 to their Strength and -1 to their Wisdom.
