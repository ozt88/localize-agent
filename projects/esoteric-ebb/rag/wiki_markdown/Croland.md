# Croland

Croland is mentioned by Visken as a bookbinder residing in Waterlane View of Tolstad, related to the quest Phoenix Folio. A poster can be find in South Tolstad:

"S. Yêlan & Sons. BINDERS & LITHOGRAPHERS. Everything from designing to engraving. ACCOUNT - BOOKS - MANUFACTURING. Waterlane View 6th, LW."
