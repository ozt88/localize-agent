# Coast War

A nearly century spanning conflict fought between the great nations of the Arcane Era. It was unimaginably destructive, with around a half of humankind perishing during it.

The Journal definition of Coast War appears after succeeding a DC 9 Strength check and reads: "Coast War - the great war. A conflict between the Arcanists of Reeds, as well as every other nation connected with them across the Coast. A horrible waste of human lives, which claimed millions of lives. There was no honor in this fighting. This you know."

## History

The Coast War is generally accepted by scholars to have begun in 398 AE, after the assassination of Princess Ilona. It lasted for 83 years, with it finally concluding in 481 AE with the Fall of Thazen.
