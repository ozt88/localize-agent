# Spectral Halberd

The Spectral Halberd is a Weapon obtained after inserting the Jacinth into the Whitemane Shrine in the South Caverns.

## In-game Description

A ghostly weapon, existing somewhere in-between the astral and material planes. However, it is sadly not really materially-bound. Meaning, it won't do any damage if you swing it. Damn.

## Effects

Equipping the Spectral Halberd grants the Cleric +1 to their Intelligence and Wisdom.
