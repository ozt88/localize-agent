# Lord Patriarch Âkesam

Lord Âkesam is the current Lord Patriarch of Norvik, the commander of city forces and highest-ranking cleric of Urth. He is the leader of the Party Of Norvik.

He was appointed 30 years prior to the events of the game, when the gods were still alive. He is now over 80 years old, and is said to be withdrawn, off-loading administration of the city to the magistrates.
