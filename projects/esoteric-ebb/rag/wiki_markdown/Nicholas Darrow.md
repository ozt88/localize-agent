# Nicholas Darrow

Nicholas Darrow is the Coinlord of Norvik and the magistrate of Tolstad. As a Coinlord, he handles minting, trade, financing of public works, and tithes. He is a great grandson of Borgo Darrow, the legendary first paladin of Urth.

Attempting to cast a mind-altering spell, such as Charm Person on Darrow will cause him to remark on it, and call Ettir up, who attacks you for 1d4 damage. This is because he is wearing Iron-Will Ring, which negates any mind-altering spells.
