# The Azgal Chant

The Azgal Chant is a piece of Literature. It is a local Azgalian newspaper.

## In-game Description

A low quality copper press paper with dwarven iconography on the corner. This is printed with love and care, and the articles are written with the working class in mind at every step. It's extremely radical, and highly Azgalian.

## Effects

Equipping The Azgal Chant causes the Cleric to get a +1 to their Wisdom.

## Trivia

* The titles on the item read: "Local rally stopped by fascist landlord" and "The Manifesto: What we learn from the Hedonists."
