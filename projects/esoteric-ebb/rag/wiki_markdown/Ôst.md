# Ôst

Ôst is a kobold in employment of the mortician Visken, for whom he acts as a guard. He can be found sitting at the exit from the upper lair of Visken's Lair.

## Description

Ôst is a kobold rogue who proudly serves Visken. Though it is apparent that neither Visken or Ôst want to portray the employment in terms of servitude, it is insinuated that their relationship is close to being of master and servant rather than employer and employee. This may be a reference to

Ôst thinks highly of his work providing security to Visken's establishment and demands respect as such, partially as a show of strength necessary for his line of work.

In conversation, he is naturally antagonistic and though he lets The Cleric question him, he does not appreciate it and will respond in stern retorts. He isn't always unwitty, though his speech may come off as simple and lacking proper grammar.

## Background

Ôst was born from an egg which Visken bought believing it is a basilisk egg. Both of them insist there is no paternal relationship in this, and Ôst works for Visken out of free will. Like his employer, he wants to vote for the Party of Norvik, despite their human-centric agenda.
