# Tisereldanour

Tisereldanour was a "great" Green Dragon and a draconic ruler. She ruled the area of the Illym Forest. Her death marked the end of the Draconic Civil War and began the Arcanist rule in Reeds (then Old Diminor).

Tiseraldanour was slain by Doug Doulin Dim.
