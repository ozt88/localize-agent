# Fourth Apprentice of Jor

The Fourth Apprentice of Jor, or the Nameless Apprentice, was one of Jor's original apprentices.

During the Coast War, the Apprentice was imprisoned in Thazen and used as fuel to drive the Haagin-Dim Coalition into near complete destruction.

The Apprentice's original name was cursed, so that anyone who heard it would laugh until they died.
