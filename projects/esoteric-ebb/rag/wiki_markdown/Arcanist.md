# Arcanist

Arcanist is one of six possible feats that can be obtained from the quest Election Week. It is related to the Arcanist political party, and is associated with Intelligence.

## Effects

The Arcanist feat makes it so that whenever the Cleric uses their last spell slot of any level, they roll a d100. If the result of the roll is less than the Cleric's natural intelligence, the spent spell slot will be regained.
