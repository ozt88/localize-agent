# Norvik

The central city of Askanii.

The Journal definition of Norvik appears after succeeding a DC 10 Strength check and reads: "Norvik - your home city. Founded by your god towards the end of the Coast War. It is built upon several layers of ancient ruins." An alternative definition appears after succeeding a DC 10 Intelligence check and reads: "Norvik - the central city of humanity. It was founded upon the end of The Reconquest in 468 AE, exactly seventy years ago."

The currency of the city is the Norvikian Crown. It is often represented with a symbol of a Great Perch.

## History

Norvik was founded by the god Urth in 468 AE, upon the end of The Reconquest.

After Urth's death, the city transitioned from Magocracy to democracy. Universal suffrage was given to any inhabitants of Norvikian soil as of the 1st of Ingarmi, 26 Postwar Era.

## Districts

The city of Norvik is separated into 5 districts:
* Tolstad
* The Palace
* Housing
* North Wall
* Dvârgby

Each district is rules by a magistrate; the Coinlord, Lawlord, Homelord, Shieldlord, and Foodlord.

There are also three "unofficial" districts, such as Ôstragârd (defined as "anything on the inland side of the river that’s not part of the North Wall"), and The Hills (defined as the hills on the western side of the river. It is also known as the halfling district).
