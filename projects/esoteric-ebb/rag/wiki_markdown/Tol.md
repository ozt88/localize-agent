# Tol

Tol was the brother of god Urth. He was a mythical explorer of Askan who founded several towns along the River Torna, including Tolstad. He brought the Weapon Tol's Sword with him on his travels and the standard Urthguard blade is modeled after it.

It is uncertain if he achieved divinity like his brother.

He has never returned from his last expedition to the Infinite Wastes. The holiday Tol's Leaving is observing this historical event.
